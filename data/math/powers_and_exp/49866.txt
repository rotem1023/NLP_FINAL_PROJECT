Problem:
Compute
$e^\left(
\begin{array}{cccc}
 -2 & -1 & -1 & 4 \\
 1 & 0 & 1 & -1 \\
 0 & 0 & -1 & 0 \\
 0 & 0 & 0 & 2 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{cccc}
 0 & -\frac{1}{e} & -\frac{1}{e} & e^2 \\
 \frac{1}{e} & \frac{2}{e} & \frac{1}{e} & -\frac{1}{e} \\
 0 & 0 & \frac{1}{e} & 0 \\
 0 & 0 & 0 & e^2 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
