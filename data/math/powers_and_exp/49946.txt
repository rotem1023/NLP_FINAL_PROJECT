Problem:
Compute
$e^\left(
\begin{array}{cccc}
 3 & -3 & 4 & -1 \\
 -3 & 3 & -4 & 1 \\
 -3 & 3 & -4 & 1 \\
 -1 & 1 & -2 & 1 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{cccc}
 2 e^2-e & 1+e-2 e^2 & -1-2 e+3 e^2 & e-e^2 \\
 1+e-2 e^2 & 2 e^2-e & 1+2 e-3 e^2 & e^2-e \\
 1+e-2 e^2 & -1-e+2 e^2 & 2+2 e-3 e^2 & e^2-e \\
 1-e & e-1 & -2 (e-1) & e \\
\end{array}
\right)$
