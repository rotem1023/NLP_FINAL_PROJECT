Problem:
Compute
$e^\left(
\begin{array}{cccc}
 2 & 4 & -4 & -8 \\
 0 & 0 & 0 & 2 \\
 0 & 2 & -2 & -6 \\
 0 & 0 & 0 & 2 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{cccc}
 e^2 & \frac{e^4-1}{e^2} & \frac{1-e^4}{e^2} & -\frac{2 \left(e^4-1\right)}{e^2} \\
 0 & 1 & 0 & e^2-1 \\
 0 & \frac{e^2-1}{e^2} & \frac{1}{e^2} & \frac{2-e^2-e^4}{e^2} \\
 0 & 0 & 0 & e^2 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
