Problem:
Compute
$e^\left(
\begin{array}{cccc}
 2 & -1 & -1 & 3 \\
 0 & -2 & 1 & 0 \\
 0 & 0 & -1 & 0 \\
 0 & 1 & -2 & -1 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{cccc}
 e^2 & \frac{1-e}{e^2} & \frac{-1+3 e-e^4}{e^2} & \frac{e^3-1}{e} \\
 0 & \frac{1}{e^2} & \frac{e-1}{e^2} & 0 \\
 0 & 0 & \frac{1}{e} & 0 \\
 0 & \frac{e-1}{e^2} & \frac{1-2 e}{e^2} & \frac{1}{e} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
