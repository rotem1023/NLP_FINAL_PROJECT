Problem:
Compute
$e^\left(
\begin{array}{cccc}
 1 & 2 & -1 & 0 \\
 0 & -1 & 1 & 0 \\
 0 & 0 & -2 & 0 \\
 1 & 1 & 0 & 2 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{cccc}
 e & e-\frac{1}{e} & \frac{1}{e^2}-\frac{1}{e} & 0 \\
 0 & \frac{1}{e} & \frac{1}{e}-\frac{1}{e^2} & 0 \\
 0 & 0 & \frac{1}{e^2} & 0 \\
 e^2-e & e^2-e & 0 & e^2 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
