Problem:
Compute
$e^\left(
\begin{array}{cccc}
 -3 & 0 & -1 & -1 \\
 -1 & 1 & 2 & 2 \\
 -3 & 1 & -4 & -6 \\
 4 & -1 & 3 & 5 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{cccc}
 0 & 0 & -\frac{1}{e^2} & -\frac{1}{e^2} \\
 -\frac{1}{e^2} & e & \frac{e^3-2}{e^2} & \frac{e^3-2}{e^2} \\
 \frac{2-e^4}{e^2} & e^2-e & \frac{3-e^3}{e^2} & \frac{3-e^3-e^4}{e^2} \\
 \frac{e^4-1}{e^2} & e-e^2 & \frac{e^3-1}{e^2} & \frac{-1+e^3+e^4}{e^2} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
