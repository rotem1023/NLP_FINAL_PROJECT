Problem:
Compute
$e^\left(
\begin{array}{cccc}
 -6 & 4 & 0 & -4 \\
 -1 & 2 & 0 & -1 \\
 -4 & 4 & -2 & -4 \\
 7 & -4 & 0 & 5 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{cccc}
 \frac{2-e^4}{e^2} & \frac{e^4-1}{e^2} & 0 & \frac{1-e^4}{e^2} \\
 e-e^2 & e^2 & 0 & e-e^2 \\
 \frac{1-e^4}{e^2} & \frac{e^4-1}{e^2} & \frac{1}{e^2} & \frac{1-e^4}{e^2} \\
 \frac{-2+e^3+e^4}{e^2} & \frac{1-e^4}{e^2} & 0 & \frac{-1+e^3+e^4}{e^2} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
