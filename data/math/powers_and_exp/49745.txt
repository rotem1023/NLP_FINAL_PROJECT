Problem:
Compute
$e^\left(
\begin{array}{cccc}
 0 & -1 & 0 & 0 \\
 0 & -1 & 0 & 0 \\
 -1 & 1 & 0 & -1 \\
 2 & -2 & 1 & 2 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{cccc}
 1 & \frac{1-e}{e} & 0 & 0 \\
 0 & \frac{1}{e} & 0 & 0 \\
 -e & e & 0 & -e \\
 2 e-1 & 1-2 e & e & 2 e \\
\end{array}
\right)$
