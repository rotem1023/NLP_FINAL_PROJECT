Problem:
Compute
$e^\left(
\begin{array}{ccc}
 -4 & 3 & -3 \\
 -5 & 4 & -3 \\
 1 & -1 & 2 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{ccc}
 \frac{2}{e}-e^2 & e^2-\frac{1}{e} & \frac{1}{e}-e^2 \\
 \frac{2}{e}-e-e^2 & -\frac{1}{e}+e+e^2 & \frac{1}{e}-e^2 \\
 e^2-e & e-e^2 & e^2 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
