Problem:
Compute
$e^\left(
\begin{array}{cccc}
 1 & -2 & -3 & 3 \\
 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 \\
 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 \\
 0 & 2 & 2 & -2 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{cccc}
 e & \frac{1-e^2}{e^2} & \frac{1-e^3}{e^2} & \frac{e^3-1}{e^2} \\
 0 & 1 & 0 & 0 \\
 0 & 0 & 1 & 0 \\
 0 & \frac{e^2-1}{e^2} & \frac{e^2-1}{e^2} & \frac{1}{e^2} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
