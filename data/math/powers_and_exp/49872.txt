Problem:
Compute
$e^\left(
\begin{array}{cccc}
 -1 & 0 & 0 & 0 \\
 0 & -1 & -1 & 1 \\
 -6 & 3 & -5 & 7 \\
 -6 & 3 & -3 & 5 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{cccc}
 \frac{1}{e} & 0 & 0 & 0 \\
 0 & \frac{1}{e} & \frac{1-e}{e^2} & \frac{e-1}{e^2} \\
 -\frac{2 \left(e^3-1\right)}{e} & \frac{e^3-1}{e} & \frac{1+e-e^4}{e^2} & \frac{-1-e+2 e^4}{e^2} \\
 -\frac{2 \left(e^3-1\right)}{e} & \frac{e^3-1}{e} & \frac{1-e^3}{e} & \frac{2 e^3-1}{e} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
