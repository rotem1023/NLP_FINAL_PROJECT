Problem:
Compute
$e^\left(
\begin{array}{cccc}
 1 & 1 & 2 & 1 \\
 -1 & 3 & 2 & 1 \\
 1 & -1 & 0 & -1 \\
 -1 & 1 & 0 & 1 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{cccc}
 e & e^2-e & e^2-1 & e-1 \\
 e-e^2 & 2 e^2-e & e^2-1 & e-1 \\
 e^2-e & e-e^2 & 1 & 1-e \\
 e-e^2 & e^2-e & 0 & e \\
\end{array}
\right)$
