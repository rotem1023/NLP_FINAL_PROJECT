Problem:
Compute
$e^\left(
\begin{array}{cccc}
 2 & 4 & 0 & -1 \\
 0 & -2 & 0 & 0 \\
 0 & 4 & 2 & 0 \\
 0 & 0 & 0 & 2 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{cccc}
 e^2 & \frac{e^4-1}{e^2} & 0 & -e^2 \\
 0 & \frac{1}{e^2} & 0 & 0 \\
 0 & \frac{e^4-1}{e^2} & e^2 & 0 \\
 0 & 0 & 0 & e^2 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
