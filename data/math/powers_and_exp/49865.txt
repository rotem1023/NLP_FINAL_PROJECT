Problem:
Compute
$e^\left(
\begin{array}{cccc}
 0 & 0 & -1 & -1 \\
 0 & 2 & 2 & 3 \\
 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 \\
 0 & 0 & 0 & -1 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{cccc}
 1 & 0 & -1 & \frac{1-e}{e} \\
 0 & e^2 & e^2-1 & \frac{e^3-1}{e} \\
 0 & 0 & 1 & 0 \\
 0 & 0 & 0 & \frac{1}{e} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
