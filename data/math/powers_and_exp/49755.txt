Problem:
Compute
$e^\left(
\begin{array}{cccc}
 1 & 0 & 0 & -3 \\
 2 & -1 & 0 & -1 \\
 -2 & 2 & 1 & 4 \\
 0 & 0 & 0 & -2 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{cccc}
 e & 0 & 0 & \frac{1-e^3}{e^2} \\
 \frac{e^2-1}{e} & \frac{1}{e} & 0 & \frac{-1+2 e-e^3}{e^2} \\
 \frac{1-e^2}{e} & \frac{e^2-1}{e} & e & \frac{2 \left(e^2-1\right)}{e} \\
 0 & 0 & 0 & \frac{1}{e^2} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
