Problem:
Compute
$e^\left(
\begin{array}{cccc}
 0 & 0 & 0 & 2 \\
 0 & 0 & 0 & -2 \\
 -1 & 1 & 1 & 1 \\
 0 & 0 & 0 & -2 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{cccc}
 1 & 0 & 0 & \frac{e^2-1}{e^2} \\
 0 & 1 & 0 & \frac{1-e^2}{e^2} \\
 1-e & e-1 & e & \frac{-1+2 e^2-e^3}{e^2} \\
 0 & 0 & 0 & \frac{1}{e^2} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
