Problem:
Compute
$e^\left(
\begin{array}{ccc}
 1 & 1 & 1 \\
 3 & 3 & 5 \\
 -3 & -3 & -5 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{ccc}
 e & e-1 & e-1 \\
 e-\frac{1}{e^2} & 1-\frac{1}{e^2}+e & 1-\frac{2}{e^2}+e \\
 \frac{1}{e^2}-e & \frac{1}{e^2}-e & \frac{2}{e^2}-e \\
\end{array}
\right)$
