Problem:
Compute
$e^\left(
\begin{array}{cccc}
 0 & -2 & 5 & -3 \\
 -1 & 1 & -3 & 3 \\
 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 \\
 1 & 1 & -1 & -1 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{cccc}
 \frac{2}{e} & \frac{2-e^3}{e} & \frac{-5+e+2 e^3}{e} & \frac{1-e^3}{e} \\
 -\frac{1}{e} & \frac{e^3-1}{e} & \frac{3+e-2 e^3}{e} & \frac{e^3-1}{e} \\
 0 & 0 & 1 & 0 \\
 \frac{1}{e} & \frac{1}{e} & \frac{e-3}{e} & \frac{1}{e} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
