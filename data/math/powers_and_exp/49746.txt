Problem:
Compute
$e^\left(
\begin{array}{cccc}
 2 & 0 & -1 & -1 \\
 3 & -1 & -3 & -2 \\
 4 & 0 & -3 & -1 \\
 -3 & 0 & 3 & 1 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{cccc}
 2 e & 0 & -e & -e \\
 \frac{e^3-1}{e^2} & \frac{1}{e} & \frac{1-e^3}{e^2} & \frac{1-e^2}{e} \\
 \frac{2 e^3-1}{e^2} & 0 & \frac{1-e^3}{e^2} & -e \\
 \frac{1-e^3}{e^2} & 0 & \frac{e^3-1}{e^2} & e \\
\end{array}
\right)$
