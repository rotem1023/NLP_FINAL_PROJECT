Problem:
Compute
$e^\left(
\begin{array}{cccc}
 0 & -1 & 1 & 0 \\
 2 & -4 & 2 & 0 \\
 2 & -3 & 1 & 0 \\
 4 & -9 & 6 & 1 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{cccc}
 1 & \frac{1}{e}-1 & 1-\frac{1}{e} & 0 \\
 1-\frac{1}{e^2} & \frac{2}{e^2}-1 & 1-\frac{1}{e^2} & 0 \\
 1-\frac{1}{e^2} & -1+\frac{2}{e^2}-\frac{1}{e} & 1-\frac{1}{e^2}+\frac{1}{e} & 0 \\
 -1-\frac{1}{e^2}+2 e & 1+\frac{2}{e^2}+\frac{1}{e}-4 e & -1-\frac{1}{e^2}-\frac{1}{e}+3 e & e \\
\end{array}
\right)$
