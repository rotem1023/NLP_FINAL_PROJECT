Problem:
Compute
$e^\left(
\begin{array}{cccc}
 2 & -4 & -2 & 4 \\
 3 & -6 & -2 & 5 \\
 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 \\
 3 & -6 & -2 & 5 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{cccc}
 e^2 & -2 \left(e^2-1\right) & 1-e^2 & 2 \left(e^2-1\right) \\
 \frac{e^3-1}{e} & \frac{2+e-2 e^3}{e} & 1-e^2 & \frac{-1-e+2 e^3}{e} \\
 0 & 0 & 1 & 0 \\
 \frac{e^3-1}{e} & -\frac{2 \left(e^3-1\right)}{e} & 1-e^2 & \frac{2 e^3-1}{e} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
