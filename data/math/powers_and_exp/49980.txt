Problem:
Compute
$e^\left(
\begin{array}{ccc}
 -2 & -1 & 1 \\
 2 & 3 & -3 \\
 2 & 5 & -5 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{ccc}
 \frac{1}{e^2} & -\frac{1}{e^2} & \frac{1}{e^2} \\
 \frac{e^2-1}{e^2} & 2 & -1 \\
 \frac{e^2-1}{e^2} & \frac{2 e^2-1}{e^2} & \frac{1-e^2}{e^2} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
