Problem:
Compute
$e^\left(
\begin{array}{cccc}
 0 & -4 & -1 & -2 \\
 0 & 2 & 0 & 0 \\
 0 & 2 & 0 & 0 \\
 0 & 1 & 0 & 2 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{cccc}
 1 & -3 \left(e^2-1\right) & -1 & 1-e^2 \\
 0 & e^2 & 0 & 0 \\
 0 & e^2-1 & 1 & 0 \\
 0 & e^2 & 0 & e^2 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
