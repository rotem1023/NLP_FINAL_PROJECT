Problem:
Compute
$e^\left(
\begin{array}{cccc}
 1 & 3 & 0 & -3 \\
 -1 & 1 & 0 & 1 \\
 -1 & -4 & 2 & 5 \\
 -1 & 3 & 0 & -1 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{cccc}
 e & \frac{e^3-1}{e^2} & 0 & \frac{1-e^3}{e^2} \\
 e-e^2 & e & 0 & e^2-e \\
 -e^2 & \frac{1-e^4}{e^2} & e^2 & \frac{2 e^4-1}{e^2} \\
 e-e^2 & \frac{e^3-1}{e^2} & 0 & \frac{1-e^3+e^4}{e^2} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
