Problem:
Compute
$e^\left(
\begin{array}{cccc}
 -1 & 2 & 0 & 0 \\
 0 & 1 & 0 & 0 \\
 1 & -4 & -2 & 0 \\
 -1 & 1 & 0 & -2 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{cccc}
 \frac{1}{e} & \frac{e^2-1}{e} & 0 & 0 \\
 0 & e & 0 & 0 \\
 \frac{e-1}{e^2} & \frac{2-e-e^3}{e^2} & \frac{1}{e^2} & 0 \\
 \frac{1-e}{e^2} & \frac{e-1}{e^2} & 0 & \frac{1}{e^2} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
