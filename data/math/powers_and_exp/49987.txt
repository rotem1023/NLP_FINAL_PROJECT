Problem:
Compute
$e^\left(
\begin{array}{cccc}
 -2 & 1 & -1 & 0 \\
 0 & -1 & -1 & 0 \\
 -1 & 1 & -2 & 0 \\
 2 & -1 & -1 & -2 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{cccc}
 \frac{e-1}{e^2} & \frac{1}{e^2} & \frac{1-e}{e^2} & 0 \\
 \frac{e-2}{e^2} & \frac{2}{e^2} & \frac{1-e}{e^2} & 0 \\
 -\frac{1}{e^2} & \frac{1}{e^2} & \frac{1}{e^2} & 0 \\
 \frac{1}{e} & -\frac{1}{e^2} & \frac{1-e}{e^2} & \frac{1}{e^2} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
