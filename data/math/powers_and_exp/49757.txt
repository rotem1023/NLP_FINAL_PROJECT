Problem:
Compute
$e^\left(
\begin{array}{cccc}
 -1 & 2 & 0 & 0 \\
 0 & 1 & 0 & 0 \\
 -3 & 3 & 2 & 0 \\
 -3 & 1 & 3 & -1 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{cccc}
 \frac{1}{e} & \frac{e^2-1}{e} & 0 & 0 \\
 0 & e & 0 & 0 \\
 \frac{1-e^3}{e} & \frac{e^3-1}{e} & e^2 & 0 \\
 \frac{1-e^3}{e} & e^2-e & \frac{e^3-1}{e} & \frac{1}{e} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
