Problem:
Find the cross product of the following vectors:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 \frac{59}{7} \\
 \frac{43}{7} \\
 -\frac{6}{7} \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 \frac{68}{7} \\
 \frac{29}{7} \\
 5 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 \frac{1679}{49} \\
 -\frac{2473}{49} \\
 -\frac{1213}{49} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
