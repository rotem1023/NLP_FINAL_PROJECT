Problem:
Find the cross product of the following vectors:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 \frac{43}{10} \\
 \frac{44}{5} \\
 \frac{71}{10} \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 4 \\
 \frac{13}{5} \\
 -\frac{17}{2} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -\frac{4663}{50} \\
 \frac{1299}{20} \\
 -\frac{1201}{50} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
