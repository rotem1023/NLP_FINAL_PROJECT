Problem:
Find the cross product of the following vectors:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -\frac{26}{3} \\
 -\frac{17}{3} \\
 -\frac{20}{3} \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -\frac{28}{3} \\
 \frac{20}{3} \\
 \frac{1}{3} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 \frac{383}{9} \\
 \frac{586}{9} \\
 -\frac{332}{3} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
