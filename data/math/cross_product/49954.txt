Problem:
Find the cross product of the following vectors:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -\frac{11}{2} \\
 \frac{21}{4} \\
 -\frac{35}{4} \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 \frac{5}{2} \\
 -\frac{31}{4} \\
 7 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -\frac{497}{16} \\
 \frac{133}{8} \\
 \frac{59}{2} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
