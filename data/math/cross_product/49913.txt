Problem:
Find the cross product of the following vectors:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -\frac{8}{5} \\
 -\frac{21}{5} \\
 \frac{11}{5} \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 \frac{49}{5} \\
 -4 \\
 -\frac{37}{5} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 \frac{997}{25} \\
 \frac{243}{25} \\
 \frac{1189}{25} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
