Problem:
Find the cross product of the following vectors:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -\frac{22}{3} \\
 \frac{22}{3} \\
 \frac{23}{9} \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 \frac{47}{9} \\
 -\frac{11}{3} \\
 \frac{37}{9} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 \frac{1067}{27} \\
 \frac{3523}{81} \\
 -\frac{308}{27} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
