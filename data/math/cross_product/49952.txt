Problem:
Find the cross product of the following vectors:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 \frac{3}{2} \\
 \frac{34}{5} \\
 -\frac{28}{5} \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -\frac{12}{5} \\
 -\frac{33}{10} \\
 9 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 \frac{1068}{25} \\
 -\frac{3}{50} \\
 \frac{1137}{100} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
