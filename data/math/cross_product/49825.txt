Problem:

$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -3 \\
 \frac{17}{9} \\
 \frac{16}{3} \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 \frac{67}{9} \\
 \frac{82}{9} \\
 -\frac{29}{9} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -\frac{4429}{81} \\
 \frac{811}{27} \\
 -\frac{3353}{81} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
