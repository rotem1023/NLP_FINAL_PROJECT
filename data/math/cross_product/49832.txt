Problem:

$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -6 \\
 -\frac{49}{5} \\
 -\frac{31}{5} \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -\frac{49}{5} \\
 \frac{7}{5} \\
 \frac{12}{5} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -\frac{371}{25} \\
 \frac{1879}{25} \\
 -\frac{2611}{25} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
