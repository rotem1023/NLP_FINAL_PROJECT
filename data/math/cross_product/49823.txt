Problem:
Find the cross product of the following vectors:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -\frac{4}{3} \\
 \frac{49}{6} \\
 \frac{20}{3} \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -\frac{11}{2} \\
 \frac{29}{3} \\
 -\frac{49}{6} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -\frac{4721}{36} \\
 -\frac{428}{9} \\
 \frac{1153}{36} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
