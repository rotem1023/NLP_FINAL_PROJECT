Problem:
Find the cross product of the following vectors:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 \frac{37}{7} \\
 \frac{6}{7} \\
 -3 \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -\frac{29}{7} \\
 \frac{45}{7} \\
 \frac{17}{7} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 \frac{1047}{49} \\
 -\frac{20}{49} \\
 \frac{1839}{49} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
