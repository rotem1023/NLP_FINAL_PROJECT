Problem:
Find the cross product of the following vectors:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 \frac{15}{2} \\
 1 \\
 -3 \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -4 \\
 -\frac{9}{2} \\
 -\frac{9}{2} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -18 \\
 \frac{183}{4} \\
 -\frac{119}{4} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
