Problem:
Find the cross product of the following vectors:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -\frac{49}{8} \\
 -\frac{7}{2} \\
 -\frac{13}{2} \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 \frac{11}{4} \\
 -\frac{19}{8} \\
 -\frac{13}{8} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -\frac{39}{4} \\
 -\frac{1781}{64} \\
 \frac{1547}{64} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
