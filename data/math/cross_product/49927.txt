Problem:
Find the cross product of the following vectors:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 \frac{9}{7} \\
 \frac{46}{7} \\
 \frac{62}{7} \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -\frac{29}{7} \\
 -\frac{5}{7} \\
 -\frac{53}{7} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -\frac{304}{7} \\
 -\frac{1321}{49} \\
 \frac{1289}{49} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
