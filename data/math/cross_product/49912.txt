Problem:
Find the cross product of the following vectors:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -\frac{31}{5} \\
 -\frac{2}{5} \\
 9 \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 0 \\
 -\frac{17}{5} \\
 \frac{8}{5} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 \frac{749}{25} \\
 \frac{248}{25} \\
 \frac{527}{25} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
