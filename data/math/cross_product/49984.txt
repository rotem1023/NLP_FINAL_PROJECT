Problem:

$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -\frac{9}{2} \\
 -\frac{7}{2} \\
 \frac{14}{3} \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -\frac{25}{6} \\
 \frac{13}{2} \\
 1 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -\frac{203}{6} \\
 -\frac{269}{18} \\
 -\frac{263}{6} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
