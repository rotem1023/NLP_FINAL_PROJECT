Problem:
Find the cross product of the following vectors:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 7 \\
 0 \\
 -\frac{9}{2} \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -7 \\
 -3 \\
 -\frac{7}{2} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -\frac{27}{2} \\
 56 \\
 -21 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
