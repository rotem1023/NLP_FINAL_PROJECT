Problem:
Find the cross product of the following vectors:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 \frac{81}{16} \\
 -\frac{77}{16} \\
 \frac{113}{16} \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -\frac{145}{16} \\
 \frac{151}{16} \\
 \frac{59}{16} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -\frac{10803}{128} \\
 -\frac{5291}{64} \\
 \frac{533}{128} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
