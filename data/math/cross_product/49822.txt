Problem:
Find the cross product of the following vectors:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -4 \\
 -4 \\
 \frac{11}{2} \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -\frac{1}{2} \\
 -\frac{3}{2} \\
 5 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -\frac{47}{4} \\
 \frac{69}{4} \\
 4 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
