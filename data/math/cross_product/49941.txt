Problem:
Find the cross product of the following vectors:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 \frac{13}{2} \\
 \frac{7}{2} \\
 \frac{3}{2} \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 \frac{5}{4} \\
 -\frac{5}{2} \\
 -\frac{3}{2} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -\frac{3}{2} \\
 \frac{93}{8} \\
 -\frac{165}{8} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
