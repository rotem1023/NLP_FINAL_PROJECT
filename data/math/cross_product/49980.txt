Problem:
Find the cross product of the following vectors:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 \frac{11}{10} \\
 -\frac{53}{10} \\
 \frac{57}{10} \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 \frac{15}{2} \\
 \frac{9}{2} \\
 \frac{24}{5} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -\frac{5109}{100} \\
 \frac{3747}{100} \\
 \frac{447}{10} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
