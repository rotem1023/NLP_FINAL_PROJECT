Problem:
Find the cross product of the following vectors:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 \frac{8}{3} \\
 -\frac{8}{3} \\
 -\frac{2}{3} \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 \frac{4}{3} \\
 \frac{4}{3} \\
 \frac{10}{3} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -8 \\
 -\frac{88}{9} \\
 \frac{64}{9} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
