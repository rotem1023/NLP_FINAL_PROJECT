Problem:
Find the cross product of the following vectors:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -\frac{35}{8} \\
 -\frac{7}{16} \\
 -\frac{23}{8} \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 \frac{87}{16} \\
 -\frac{67}{8} \\
 \frac{7}{16} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -\frac{6213}{256} \\
 -\frac{439}{32} \\
 \frac{9989}{256} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
