Problem:

$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 \frac{193}{100} \\
 -\frac{361}{50} \\
 \frac{69}{10} \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -\frac{17}{5} \\
 \frac{13}{10} \\
 -\frac{59}{100} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -\frac{23551}{5000} \\
 -\frac{223213}{10000} \\
 -\frac{22039}{1000} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
