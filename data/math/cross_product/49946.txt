Problem:
Find the cross product of the following vectors:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -\frac{9}{2} \\
 -\frac{3}{2} \\
 5 \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 \frac{15}{2} \\
 3 \\
 -\frac{1}{2} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -\frac{57}{4} \\
 \frac{141}{4} \\
 -\frac{9}{4} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
