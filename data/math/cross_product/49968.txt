Problem:
Find the cross product of the following vectors:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 \frac{5}{3} \\
 -\frac{74}{9} \\
 -\frac{17}{9} \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 \frac{10}{3} \\
 -\frac{20}{9} \\
 -4 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 \frac{2324}{81} \\
 \frac{10}{27} \\
 \frac{640}{27} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
