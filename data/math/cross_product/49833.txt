Problem:
Find the cross product of the following vectors:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 \frac{26}{3} \\
 -\frac{4}{3} \\
 -\frac{7}{3} \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 \frac{16}{3} \\
 -5 \\
 \frac{29}{3} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -\frac{221}{9} \\
 -\frac{866}{9} \\
 -\frac{326}{9} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
