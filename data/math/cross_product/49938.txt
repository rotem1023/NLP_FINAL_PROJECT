Problem:
Find the cross product of the following vectors:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -9 \\
 \frac{93}{10} \\
 -4 \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -\frac{49}{10} \\
 -\frac{27}{5} \\
 -\frac{4}{5} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -\frac{726}{25} \\
 \frac{62}{5} \\
 \frac{9417}{100} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
