Problem:
Find the cross product of the following vectors:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -\frac{39}{4} \\
 -\frac{5}{2} \\
 \frac{15}{2} \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 \frac{21}{4} \\
 -\frac{15}{2} \\
 \frac{21}{4} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 \frac{345}{8} \\
 \frac{1449}{16} \\
 \frac{345}{4} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
