Problem:
Find the cross product of the following vectors:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -6 \\
 -\frac{11}{5} \\
 \frac{21}{5} \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 \frac{16}{5} \\
 -\frac{48}{5} \\
 -\frac{46}{5} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 \frac{1514}{25} \\
 -\frac{1044}{25} \\
 \frac{1616}{25} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
