Problem:
Find the cross product of the following vectors:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -\frac{20}{3} \\
 \frac{4}{3} \\
 \frac{22}{3} \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -\frac{29}{3} \\
 -\frac{16}{3} \\
 \frac{17}{3} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 \frac{140}{3} \\
 -\frac{298}{9} \\
 \frac{436}{9} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
