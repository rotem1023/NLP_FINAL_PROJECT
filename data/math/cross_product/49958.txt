Problem:
Find the cross product of the following vectors:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -\frac{7}{4} \\
 -\frac{55}{8} \\
 \frac{17}{4} \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -\frac{17}{2} \\
 \frac{1}{2} \\
 \frac{9}{4} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -\frac{563}{32} \\
 -\frac{515}{16} \\
 -\frac{949}{16} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
