Problem:

$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -\frac{77}{50} \\
 -\frac{29}{100} \\
 \frac{17}{2} \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 \frac{129}{100} \\
 -\frac{699}{100} \\
 -\frac{327}{50} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 \frac{153279}{2500} \\
 \frac{4467}{5000} \\
 \frac{111387}{10000} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
