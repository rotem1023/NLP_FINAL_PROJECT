$\left(
\begin{array}{c}

\end{array}
\right)$ and
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 0 \\

 \frac{13}{4} \\
\end{array}
\right)$

$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -\frac{1279}{16} \\
