Problem:
Find the cross product of the following vectors:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -\frac{11}{6} \\
 -\frac{1}{3} \\
 \frac{9}{2} \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 \frac{13}{2} \\
 2 \\
 \frac{1}{6} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -\frac{163}{18} \\
 \frac{266}{9} \\
 -\frac{3}{2} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
