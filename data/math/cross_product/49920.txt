Problem:
Find the cross product of the following vectors:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 \frac{23}{9} \\
 -\frac{32}{9} \\
 -\frac{10}{3} \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 \frac{2}{3} \\
 -\frac{79}{9} \\
 \frac{23}{9} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -\frac{3106}{81} \\
 -\frac{709}{81} \\
 -\frac{1625}{81} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
