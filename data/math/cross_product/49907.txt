Problem:
Find the cross product of the following vectors:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -\frac{37}{16} \\
 -\frac{53}{16} \\
 -\frac{9}{8} \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -\frac{69}{16} \\
 -\frac{131}{16} \\
 \frac{17}{2} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -\frac{4783}{128} \\
 \frac{3137}{128} \\
 \frac{595}{128} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
