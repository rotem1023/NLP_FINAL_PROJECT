Problem:
Find the cross product of the following vectors:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 \frac{121}{16} \\
 \frac{37}{4} \\
 \frac{79}{8} \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 1 \\
 -\frac{53}{16} \\
 \frac{39}{8} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 \frac{9959}{128} \\
 -\frac{3455}{128} \\
 -\frac{8781}{256} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
