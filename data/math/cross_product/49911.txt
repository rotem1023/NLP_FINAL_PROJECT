Problem:

$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -\frac{17}{5} \\
 6 \\
 -\frac{17}{5} \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 \frac{21}{5} \\
 \frac{9}{5} \\
 \frac{49}{5} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 \frac{1623}{25} \\
 \frac{476}{25} \\
 -\frac{783}{25} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
