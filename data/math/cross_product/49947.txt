Problem:
Find the cross product of the following vectors:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 \frac{49}{5} \\
 -\frac{26}{5} \\
 \frac{49}{10} \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 \frac{7}{5} \\
 -\frac{44}{5} \\
 -\frac{51}{10} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 \frac{1741}{25} \\
 \frac{1421}{25} \\
 -\frac{1974}{25} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
