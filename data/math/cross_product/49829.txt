Problem:
Find the cross product of the following vectors:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -\frac{23}{5} \\
 -\frac{99}{10} \\
 \frac{11}{5} \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 \frac{19}{2} \\
 \frac{5}{2} \\
 \frac{73}{10} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -\frac{7777}{100} \\
 \frac{1362}{25} \\
 \frac{1651}{20} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
