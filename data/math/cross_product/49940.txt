Problem:
Find the cross product of the following vectors:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -3 \\
 \frac{13}{3} \\
 \frac{5}{3} \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 \frac{17}{3} \\
 \frac{11}{3} \\
 -\frac{22}{3} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -\frac{341}{9} \\
 -\frac{113}{9} \\
 -\frac{320}{9} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
