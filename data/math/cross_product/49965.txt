Problem:

$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 \frac{477}{50} \\
 \frac{551}{100} \\
 \frac{67}{20} \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 \frac{657}{100} \\
 -\frac{467}{100} \\
 -\frac{43}{50} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 \frac{109059}{10000} \\
 \frac{302139}{10000} \\
 -\frac{32301}{400} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
