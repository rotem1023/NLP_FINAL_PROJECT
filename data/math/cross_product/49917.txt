Problem:
Find the cross product of the following vectors:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -\frac{4}{7} \\
 6 \\
 -4 \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 \frac{15}{7} \\
 7 \\
 -\frac{61}{7} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -\frac{170}{7} \\
 -\frac{664}{49} \\
 -\frac{118}{7} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
