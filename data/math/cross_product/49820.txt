Problem:
Find the cross product of the following vectors:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 \frac{11}{2} \\
 \frac{9}{2} \\
 -3 \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 \frac{13}{2} \\
 \frac{17}{2} \\
 \frac{7}{2} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 \frac{165}{4} \\
 -\frac{155}{4} \\
 \frac{35}{2} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
