Problem:
Find the cross product of the following vectors:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 \frac{3}{4} \\
 \frac{153}{16} \\
 -\frac{79}{16} \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -\frac{41}{16} \\
 -\frac{41}{16} \\
 3 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 \frac{4105}{256} \\
 \frac{2663}{256} \\
 \frac{5781}{256} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
