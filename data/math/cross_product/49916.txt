Problem:
Find the cross product of the following vectors:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -\frac{17}{16} \\
 2 \\
 \frac{45}{16} \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 \frac{31}{16} \\
 -\frac{41}{8} \\
 3 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 \frac{2613}{128} \\
 \frac{2211}{256} \\
 \frac{201}{128} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
