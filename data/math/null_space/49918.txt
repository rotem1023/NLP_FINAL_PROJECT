Problem:
Give a list of vectors that forms a basis for the null space of the following matrix (round your answer to three decimal places):
$\left(
\begin{array}{ccccc}
 -7 & 1 & -1 & -7 & -1 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
${\{-1.,0.,0.,0.,7.\}, \{-1.,0.,0.,1.,0.\}, \{-1.,0.,7.,0.,0.\}, \{1.,7.,0.,0.,0.\}}$
