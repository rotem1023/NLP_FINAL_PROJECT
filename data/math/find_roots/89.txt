Problem:
Find all roots of the following function: $\frac{9 x^5-4}{\log \left(8 x^4+2\right)}=0$
Answer:
$\left\{\left\{x\to \left(-\frac{2}{3}\right)^{2/5}\right\},\left\{x\to \left(\frac{2}{3}\right)^{2/5}\right\},\left\{x\to -\sqrt[5]{-1} \left(\frac{2}{3}\right)^{2/5}\right\},\left\{x\to -(-1)^{3/5} \left(\frac{2}{3}\right)^{2/5}\right\},\left\{x\to (-1)^{4/5} \left(\frac{2}{3}\right)^{2/5}\right\}\right\}$
