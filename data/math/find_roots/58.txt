Problem:
Find all roots of the following function: $\sin \left(\frac{9}{2}-\frac{3 x}{2}\right)=0$
Answer:
$\left\{\left\{x\to \fbox{$\frac{1}{3} (4 \pi  c_1+9)\text{ if }c_1\in \mathbb{Z}$}\right\},\left\{x\to \fbox{$\frac{1}{3} (2 (2 \pi  c_1+\pi )+9)\text{ if }c_1\in \mathbb{Z}$}\right\}\right\}$
