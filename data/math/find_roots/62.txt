Problem:
Find all roots of the following function: $\frac{-x^4-\frac{11}{2}}{\sqrt[3]{\frac{17 x}{2}+\frac{7}{2}}}=0$
Answer:
$\left\{\left\{x\to -\sqrt[4]{-\frac{11}{2}}\right\},\left\{x\to -i \sqrt[4]{-\frac{11}{2}}\right\},\left\{x\to i \sqrt[4]{-\frac{11}{2}}\right\},\left\{x\to \sqrt[4]{-\frac{11}{2}}\right\}\right\}$
