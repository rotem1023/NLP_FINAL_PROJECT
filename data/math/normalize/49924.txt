Problem:
Normalize the following vector:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 \frac{17}{16} \\
 -\frac{47}{16} \\
 -\frac{45}{16} \\
 -\frac{1}{2} \\
 -\frac{47}{16} \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 \frac{17}{2 \sqrt{1699}} \\
 -\frac{47}{2 \sqrt{1699}} \\
 -\frac{45}{2 \sqrt{1699}} \\
 -\frac{4}{\sqrt{1699}} \\
 -\frac{47}{2 \sqrt{1699}} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
