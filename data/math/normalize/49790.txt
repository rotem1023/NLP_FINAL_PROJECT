Problem:
Normalize the following vector:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 \frac{19}{16} \\
 \frac{17}{8} \\
 -\frac{5}{16} \\
 \frac{41}{16} \\
 -\frac{15}{8} \\
 \frac{27}{16} \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 \frac{19}{2 \sqrt{1213}} \\
 \frac{17}{\sqrt{1213}} \\
 -\frac{5}{2 \sqrt{1213}} \\
 \frac{41}{2 \sqrt{1213}} \\
 -\frac{15}{\sqrt{1213}} \\
 \frac{27}{2 \sqrt{1213}} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
