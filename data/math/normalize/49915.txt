Problem:
Normalize the following vector:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -\frac{16}{7} \\
 -\frac{10}{7} \\
 -\frac{1}{7} \\
 -\frac{13}{7} \\
 \frac{2}{7} \\
 \frac{18}{7} \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -8 \sqrt{\frac{2}{427}} \\
 -5 \sqrt{\frac{2}{427}} \\
 -\frac{1}{\sqrt{854}} \\
 -\frac{13}{\sqrt{854}} \\
 \sqrt{\frac{2}{427}} \\
 9 \sqrt{\frac{2}{427}} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
