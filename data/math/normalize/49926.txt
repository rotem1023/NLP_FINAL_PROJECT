Problem:
Normalize the following vector:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 \frac{13}{5} \\
 \frac{9}{10} \\
 -\frac{9}{5} \\
 -\frac{3}{5} \\
 -\frac{1}{2} \\
 -3 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 13 \sqrt{\frac{2}{1021}} \\
 \frac{9}{\sqrt{2042}} \\
 -9 \sqrt{\frac{2}{1021}} \\
 -3 \sqrt{\frac{2}{1021}} \\
 -\frac{5}{\sqrt{2042}} \\
 -15 \sqrt{\frac{2}{1021}} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
