Problem:
Normalize the following vector:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 \frac{5}{2} \\
 -\frac{11}{4} \\
 \frac{3}{4} \\
 \frac{9}{4} \\
 -\frac{1}{2} \\
 -\frac{1}{2} \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 \frac{10}{\sqrt{319}} \\
 -\sqrt{\frac{11}{29}} \\
 \frac{3}{\sqrt{319}} \\
 \frac{9}{\sqrt{319}} \\
 -\frac{2}{\sqrt{319}} \\
 -\frac{2}{\sqrt{319}} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
