Problem:
Normalize the following vector:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 \frac{4}{3} \\
 -\frac{2}{3} \\
 -\frac{5}{9} \\
 -\frac{13}{9} \\
 \frac{7}{3} \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 \frac{12}{\sqrt{815}} \\
 -\frac{6}{\sqrt{815}} \\
 -\sqrt{\frac{5}{163}} \\
 -\frac{13}{\sqrt{815}} \\
 \frac{21}{\sqrt{815}} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
