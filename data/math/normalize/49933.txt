Problem:
Normalize the following vector:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -\frac{1}{5} \\
 -3 \\
 -1 \\
 -2 \\
 -\frac{4}{5} \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -\frac{1}{\sqrt{367}} \\
 -\frac{15}{\sqrt{367}} \\
 -\frac{5}{\sqrt{367}} \\
 -\frac{10}{\sqrt{367}} \\
 -\frac{4}{\sqrt{367}} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
