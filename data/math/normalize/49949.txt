Problem:
Normalize the following vector:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -\frac{14}{5} \\
 \frac{29}{10} \\
 -\frac{23}{10} \\
 -\frac{4}{5} \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -14 \sqrt{\frac{2}{1109}} \\
 \frac{29}{\sqrt{2218}} \\
 -\frac{23}{\sqrt{2218}} \\
 -4 \sqrt{\frac{2}{1109}} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
