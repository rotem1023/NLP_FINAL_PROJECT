Problem:
Normalize the following vector:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -\frac{1}{2} \\
 \frac{7}{4} \\
 \frac{5}{2} \\
 \frac{1}{4} \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -\sqrt{\frac{2}{77}} \\
 \sqrt{\frac{7}{22}} \\
 5 \sqrt{\frac{2}{77}} \\
 \frac{1}{\sqrt{154}} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
