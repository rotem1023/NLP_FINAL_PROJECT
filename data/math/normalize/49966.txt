Problem:
Normalize the following vector:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -1 \\
 \frac{3}{7} \\
 -2 \\
 1 \\
 \frac{13}{7} \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -\frac{7}{2 \sqrt{118}} \\
 \frac{3}{2 \sqrt{118}} \\
 -\frac{7}{\sqrt{118}} \\
 \frac{7}{2 \sqrt{118}} \\
 \frac{13}{2 \sqrt{118}} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
