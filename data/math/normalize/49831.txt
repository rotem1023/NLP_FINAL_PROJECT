Problem:
Normalize the following vector:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -\frac{16}{9} \\
 \frac{25}{9} \\
 \frac{17}{9} \\
 \frac{2}{9} \\
 -\frac{2}{9} \\
 \frac{5}{9} \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -\frac{16}{\sqrt{1203}} \\
 \frac{25}{\sqrt{1203}} \\
 \frac{17}{\sqrt{1203}} \\
 \frac{2}{\sqrt{1203}} \\
 -\frac{2}{\sqrt{1203}} \\
 \frac{5}{\sqrt{1203}} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
