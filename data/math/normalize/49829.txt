Problem:
Normalize the following vector:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -1 \\
 -\frac{5}{3} \\
 \frac{1}{3} \\
 -\frac{4}{3} \\
 -\frac{11}{6} \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -\frac{6}{5 \sqrt{13}} \\
 -\frac{2}{\sqrt{13}} \\
 \frac{2}{5 \sqrt{13}} \\
 -\frac{8}{5 \sqrt{13}} \\
 -\frac{11}{5 \sqrt{13}} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
