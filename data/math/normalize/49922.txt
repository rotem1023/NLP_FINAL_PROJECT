Problem:
Normalize the following vector:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -\frac{12}{7} \\
 -3 \\
 \frac{17}{7} \\
 -2 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -6 \sqrt{\frac{2}{535}} \\
 -\frac{21}{\sqrt{1070}} \\
 \frac{17}{\sqrt{1070}} \\
 -7 \sqrt{\frac{2}{535}} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
