Problem:
Normalize the following vector:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 \frac{13}{5} \\
 -\frac{8}{5} \\
 -\frac{12}{5} \\
 \frac{13}{5} \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 \sqrt{\frac{13}{42}} \\
 -4 \sqrt{\frac{2}{273}} \\
 -2 \sqrt{\frac{6}{91}} \\
 \sqrt{\frac{13}{42}} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
