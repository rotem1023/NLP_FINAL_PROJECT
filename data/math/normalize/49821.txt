Problem:
Normalize the following vector:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -\frac{9}{4} \\
 -2 \\
 -\frac{3}{4} \\
 \frac{9}{4} \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -\frac{9}{\sqrt{235}} \\
 -\frac{8}{\sqrt{235}} \\
 -\frac{3}{\sqrt{235}} \\
 \frac{9}{\sqrt{235}} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
