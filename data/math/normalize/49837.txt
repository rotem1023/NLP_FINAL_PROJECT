Problem:
Normalize the following vector:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -\frac{3}{8} \\
 \frac{25}{16} \\
 \frac{41}{16} \\
 \frac{9}{4} \\
 -\frac{7}{16} \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -2 \sqrt{\frac{3}{1229}} \\
 \frac{25}{\sqrt{3687}} \\
 \frac{41}{\sqrt{3687}} \\
 12 \sqrt{\frac{3}{1229}} \\
 -\frac{7}{\sqrt{3687}} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
