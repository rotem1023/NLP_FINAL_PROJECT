Problem:
Normalize the following vector:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -\frac{47}{16} \\
 -\frac{7}{16} \\
 -3 \\
 \frac{19}{8} \\
 -\frac{11}{16} \\
 \frac{39}{16} \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -\frac{47}{4 \sqrt{478}} \\
 -\frac{7}{4 \sqrt{478}} \\
 -6 \sqrt{\frac{2}{239}} \\
 \frac{19}{2 \sqrt{478}} \\
 -\frac{11}{4 \sqrt{478}} \\
 \frac{39}{4 \sqrt{478}} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
