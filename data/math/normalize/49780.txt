Problem:
Normalize the following vector:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 3 \\
 -2 \\
 3 \\
 3 \\
 -1 \\
 1 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 \sqrt{\frac{3}{11}} \\
 -\frac{2}{\sqrt{33}} \\
 \sqrt{\frac{3}{11}} \\
 \sqrt{\frac{3}{11}} \\
 -\frac{1}{\sqrt{33}} \\
 \frac{1}{\sqrt{33}} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
