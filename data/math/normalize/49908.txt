Problem:
Normalize the following vector:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -\frac{1}{7} \\
 -\frac{8}{7} \\
 \frac{5}{7} \\
 -\frac{19}{7} \\
 -\frac{19}{7} \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -\frac{1}{2 \sqrt{203}} \\
 -\frac{4}{\sqrt{203}} \\
 \frac{5}{2 \sqrt{203}} \\
 -\frac{19}{2 \sqrt{203}} \\
 -\frac{19}{2 \sqrt{203}} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
