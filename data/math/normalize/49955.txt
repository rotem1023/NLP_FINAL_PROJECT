Problem:
Normalize the following vector:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 2 \\
 -\frac{3}{2} \\
 \frac{5}{4} \\
 \frac{5}{8} \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 \frac{16}{5 \sqrt{21}} \\
 -\frac{4 \sqrt{\frac{3}{7}}}{5} \\
 \frac{2}{\sqrt{21}} \\
 \frac{1}{\sqrt{21}} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
