Problem:
Normalize the following vector:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -\frac{7}{3} \\
 -\frac{4}{3} \\
 \frac{4}{3} \\
 -\frac{1}{6} \\
 \frac{3}{2} \\
 -\frac{5}{6} \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -\frac{14}{\sqrt{431}} \\
 -\frac{8}{\sqrt{431}} \\
 \frac{8}{\sqrt{431}} \\
 -\frac{1}{\sqrt{431}} \\
 \frac{9}{\sqrt{431}} \\
 -\frac{5}{\sqrt{431}} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
