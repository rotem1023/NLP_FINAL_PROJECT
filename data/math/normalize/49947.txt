Problem:
Normalize the following vector:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -\frac{11}{6} \\
 -2 \\
 -2 \\
 -\frac{7}{6} \\
 \frac{5}{2} \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -\frac{11}{\sqrt{683}} \\
 -\frac{12}{\sqrt{683}} \\
 -\frac{12}{\sqrt{683}} \\
 -\frac{7}{\sqrt{683}} \\
 \frac{15}{\sqrt{683}} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
