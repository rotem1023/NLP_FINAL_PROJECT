Problem:
Normalize the following vector:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -\frac{2}{3} \\
 -\frac{7}{6} \\
 \frac{5}{6} \\
 2 \\
 -\frac{1}{3} \\
 \frac{8}{3} \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -2 \sqrt{\frac{2}{247}} \\
 -\frac{7}{\sqrt{494}} \\
 \frac{5}{\sqrt{494}} \\
 6 \sqrt{\frac{2}{247}} \\
 -\sqrt{\frac{2}{247}} \\
 8 \sqrt{\frac{2}{247}} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
