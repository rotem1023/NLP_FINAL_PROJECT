Problem:
Normalize the following vector:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 1 \\
 1 \\
 1 \\
 -2 \\
 0 \\
 1 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 \frac{1}{2 \sqrt{2}} \\
 \frac{1}{2 \sqrt{2}} \\
 \frac{1}{2 \sqrt{2}} \\
 -\frac{1}{\sqrt{2}} \\
 0 \\
 \frac{1}{2 \sqrt{2}} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
