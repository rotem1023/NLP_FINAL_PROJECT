Problem:
Normalize the following vector:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 \frac{2}{3} \\
 \frac{7}{3} \\
 1 \\
 -\frac{8}{3} \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 \frac{\sqrt{\frac{2}{7}}}{3} \\
 \frac{\sqrt{\frac{7}{2}}}{3} \\
 \frac{1}{\sqrt{14}} \\
 -\frac{4 \sqrt{\frac{2}{7}}}{3} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
