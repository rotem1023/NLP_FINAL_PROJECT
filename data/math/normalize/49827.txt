Problem:
Normalize the following vector:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 \frac{5}{7} \\
 \frac{15}{7} \\
 -3 \\
 \frac{15}{7} \\
 -\frac{2}{7} \\
 -\frac{6}{7} \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 \frac{5}{2 \sqrt{239}} \\
 \frac{15}{2 \sqrt{239}} \\
 -\frac{21}{2 \sqrt{239}} \\
 \frac{15}{2 \sqrt{239}} \\
 -\frac{1}{\sqrt{239}} \\
 -\frac{3}{\sqrt{239}} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
