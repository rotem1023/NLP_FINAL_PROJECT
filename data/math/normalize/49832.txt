Problem:
Normalize the following vector:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 \frac{1}{2} \\
 -3 \\
 \frac{3}{2} \\
 \frac{1}{2} \\
 -1 \\
 -\frac{1}{2} \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 \frac{1}{2 \sqrt{13}} \\
 -\frac{3}{\sqrt{13}} \\
 \frac{3}{2 \sqrt{13}} \\
 \frac{1}{2 \sqrt{13}} \\
 -\frac{1}{\sqrt{13}} \\
 -\frac{1}{2 \sqrt{13}} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
