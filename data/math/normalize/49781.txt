Problem:
Normalize the following vector:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 \frac{6}{5} \\
 -1 \\
 \frac{7}{5} \\
 \frac{3}{5} \\
 -\frac{7}{5} \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 \sqrt{\frac{3}{14}} \\
 -\frac{5}{2 \sqrt{42}} \\
 \frac{\sqrt{\frac{7}{6}}}{2} \\
 \frac{\sqrt{\frac{3}{14}}}{2} \\
 -\frac{\sqrt{\frac{7}{6}}}{2} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
