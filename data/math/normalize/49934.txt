Problem:
Normalize the following vector:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 \frac{37}{16} \\
 -\frac{45}{16} \\
 -\frac{7}{16} \\
 -\frac{45}{16} \\
 -\frac{19}{16} \\
 \frac{23}{8} \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 \frac{37}{\sqrt{7945}} \\
 -9 \sqrt{\frac{5}{1589}} \\
 -\sqrt{\frac{7}{1135}} \\
 -9 \sqrt{\frac{5}{1589}} \\
 -\frac{19}{\sqrt{7945}} \\
 \frac{46}{\sqrt{7945}} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
