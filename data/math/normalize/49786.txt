Problem:
Normalize the following vector:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 \frac{11}{4} \\
 -\frac{17}{8} \\
 -\frac{13}{8} \\
 -\frac{5}{8} \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 \frac{22}{\sqrt{967}} \\
 -\frac{17}{\sqrt{967}} \\
 -\frac{13}{\sqrt{967}} \\
 -\frac{5}{\sqrt{967}} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
