Problem:
Normalize the following vector:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 2 \\
 1 \\
 -1 \\
 -2 \\
 2 \\
 1 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 \frac{2}{\sqrt{15}} \\
 \frac{1}{\sqrt{15}} \\
 -\frac{1}{\sqrt{15}} \\
 -\frac{2}{\sqrt{15}} \\
 \frac{2}{\sqrt{15}} \\
 \frac{1}{\sqrt{15}} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
