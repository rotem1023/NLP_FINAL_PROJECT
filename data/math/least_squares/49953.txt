Problem:
Find the least squares vector given the matrix
$\left(
\begin{array}{ccccc}
 -2 & 2 & 3 & 2 & -3 \\
 1 & 1 & 1 & 2 & -2 \\
 -1 & -1 & -2 & 0 & 2 \\
 -2 & 3 & 3 & 0 & -1 \\
 -1 & 1 & 3 & -2 & -3 \\
 3 & 1 & 0 & 0 & -3 \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and the vector
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -1.82 \\
 -2.73 \\
 -1.04 \\
 2.26 \\
 -2.25 \\
 -0.69 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 0.758 \\
 1.271 \\
 0.573 \\
 -0.394 \\
 1.527 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
