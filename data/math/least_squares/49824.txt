Problem:
Find the least squares vector given the matrix
$\left(
\begin{array}{ccc}
 -2 & -1 & -3 \\
 -3 & 1 & 3 \\
 1 & -2 & -2 \\
 3 & 3 & -1 \\
 -3 & -3 & 1 \\
 -2 & 2 & -3 \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and the vector
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -0.2 \\
 1.32 \\
 2.39 \\
 -1.91 \\
 0.57 \\
 -1.7 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 0.037 \\
 -0.504 \\
 0.198 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
