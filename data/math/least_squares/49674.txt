Problem:
Find the least squares vector given the matrix
$\left(
\begin{array}{ccc}
 -3 & 3 & 1 \\
 0 & 2 & -3 \\
 1 & 3 & 0 \\
 -1 & 3 & 1 \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and the vector
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -2.93 \\
 2.29 \\
 1.6 \\
 -2.42 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 0.927 \\
 0.054 \\
 -0.774 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
