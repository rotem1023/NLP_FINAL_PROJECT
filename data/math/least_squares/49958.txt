Problem:
Find the least squares vector given the matrix
$\left(
\begin{array}{ccc}
 3 & 3 & 0 \\
 0 & -3 & -3 \\
 1 & 3 & 1 \\
 3 & -1 & 2 \\
 -1 & 2 & 0 \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and the vector
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 2.47 \\
 0.91 \\
 0.65 \\
 0.75 \\
 -1.6 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 0.731 \\
 0.066 \\
 -0.454 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
