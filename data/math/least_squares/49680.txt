Problem:
Find the least squares vector given the matrix
$\left(
\begin{array}{cc}
 -3 & 0 \\
 -1 & 2 \\
 3 & 2 \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and the vector
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -2.83 \\
 -0.1 \\
 -0.28 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 0.478 \\
 -0.334 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
