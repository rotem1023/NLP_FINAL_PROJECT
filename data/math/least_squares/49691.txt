Problem:
Find the least squares vector given the matrix
$\left(
\begin{array}{ccc}
 2 & -2 & -3 \\
 3 & -2 & -2 \\
 -2 & -3 & 3 \\
 1 & -3 & 1 \\
 -3 & -3 & -2 \\
 3 & 0 & -1 \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and the vector
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 0.43 \\
 0.79 \\
 -0.2 \\
 -2.37 \\
 -1.06 \\
 -0.52 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 0.009 \\
 0.258 \\
 -0.147 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
