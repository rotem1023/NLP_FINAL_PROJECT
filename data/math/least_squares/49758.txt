Problem:
Find the least squares vector given the matrix
$\left(
\begin{array}{cccc}
 0 & -1 & 1 & -2 \\
 2 & 3 & -2 & -1 \\
 1 & -3 & 2 & 3 \\
 0 & -3 & -2 & -2 \\
 -1 & 1 & 1 & 0 \\
 2 & -3 & -3 & 3 \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and the vector
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -0.19 \\
 -1.96 \\
 0.27 \\
 1.98 \\
 2.35 \\
 0.55 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -1.001 \\
 -0.343 \\
 -0.309 \\
 0.137 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
