Problem:
Find the least squares vector given the matrix
$\left(
\begin{array}{cc}
 3 & 3 \\
 0 & -1 \\
 1 & -1 \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and the vector
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -1.17 \\
 0.76 \\
 -2.31 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -1.051 \\
 0.586 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
