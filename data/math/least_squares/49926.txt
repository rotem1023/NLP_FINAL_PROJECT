Problem:
Find the least squares vector given the matrix
$\left(
\begin{array}{ccc}
 -3 & -3 & -1 \\
 -2 & 2 & 2 \\
 0 & 0 & 3 \\
 2 & 2 & -1 \\
 2 & -2 & 0 \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and the vector
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 2.78 \\
 -1.65 \\
 2.74 \\
 2.59 \\
 0.44 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 0.171 \\
 -0.425 \\
 0.146 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
