Problem:
Find the least squares vector given the matrix
$\left(
\begin{array}{cccc}
 3 & 0 & 2 & -2 \\
 3 & 1 & 2 & 1 \\
 3 & 0 & 1 & 0 \\
 -3 & 3 & 1 & 2 \\
 0 & 2 & 3 & -2 \\
 0 & 1 & -2 & -1 \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and the vector
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -0.71 \\
 2.58 \\
 1.92 \\
 -2.76 \\
 -0.81 \\
 0.33 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 0.741 \\
 -0.028 \\
 -0.319 \\
 0.373 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
