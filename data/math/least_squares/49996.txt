Problem:
Find the least squares vector given the matrix
$\left(
\begin{array}{cccc}
 -1 & -1 & 1 & 1 \\
 -2 & 3 & 0 & -2 \\
 2 & 0 & 3 & -2 \\
 0 & 3 & 0 & -1 \\
 -3 & -3 & 0 & 2 \\
 -3 & 2 & 3 & 2 \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and the vector
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 2.23 \\
 2.42 \\
 0.55 \\
 2.93 \\
 -1.01 \\
 -0.18 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -0.327 \\
 0.276 \\
 0.064 \\
 -0.557 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
