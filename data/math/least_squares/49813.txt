Problem:
Find the least squares vector given the matrix
$\left(
\begin{array}{cccc}
 2 & 1 & -1 & -1 \\
 1 & -1 & -2 & 1 \\
 -2 & 1 & 3 & 3 \\
 1 & 0 & -2 & -3 \\
 0 & 3 & 2 & -2 \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and the vector
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 1.13 \\
 -2.24 \\
 1.4 \\
 0.43 \\
 2.27 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 0.324 \\
 0.288 \\
 0.694 \\
 -0.303 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
