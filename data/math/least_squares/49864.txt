Problem:
Find the least squares vector given the matrix
$\left(
\begin{array}{cccc}
 0 & -3 & 3 & -2 \\
 -1 & 0 & 1 & 3 \\
 1 & -3 & -2 & -3 \\
 -2 & 1 & -1 & -1 \\
 -2 & -1 & -1 & -2 \\
 3 & -1 & 1 & 3 \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and the vector
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -0.2 \\
 -1.35 \\
 -1.24 \\
 0.48 \\
 1.01 \\
 1.75 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 0.215 \\
 0.225 \\
 0.091 \\
 -0.079 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
