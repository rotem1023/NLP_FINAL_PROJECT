Problem:
Find the least squares vector given the matrix
$\left(
\begin{array}{cc}
 0 & 3 \\
 0 & 0 \\
 3 & -1 \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and the vector
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -2.78 \\
 -0.32 \\
 0.82 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -0.036 \\
 -0.927 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
