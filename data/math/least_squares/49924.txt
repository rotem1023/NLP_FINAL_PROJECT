Problem:
Find the least squares vector given the matrix
$\left(
\begin{array}{ccccc}
 -1 & 0 & -1 & -2 & -2 \\
 0 & 3 & 1 & 3 & 0 \\
 -3 & 0 & 0 & 2 & -1 \\
 1 & 2 & 1 & 2 & -3 \\
 2 & 2 & 3 & -2 & 3 \\
 -2 & -2 & -1 & 3 & 0 \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and the vector
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -1.54 \\
 -0.32 \\
 -1.46 \\
 -2.1 \\
 1.69 \\
 0.85 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 0.282 \\
 -0.243 \\
 -0.126 \\
 0.149 \\
 0.702 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
