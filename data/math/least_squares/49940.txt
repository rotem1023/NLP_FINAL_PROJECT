Problem:
Find the least squares vector given the matrix
$\left(
\begin{array}{ccc}
 3 & 1 & 2 \\
 1 & 3 & 3 \\
 -1 & -2 & 0 \\
 2 & 2 & 3 \\
 2 & -2 & 1 \\
 2 & 2 & -1 \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and the vector
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -2.77 \\
 2.26 \\
 2.94 \\
 -1.27 \\
 0.92 \\
 2.36 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -0.202 \\
 0.075 \\
 -0.081 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
