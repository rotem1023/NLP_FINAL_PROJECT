Problem:
Find the least squares vector given the matrix
$\left(
\begin{array}{cccc}
 0 & 1 & 0 & 1 \\
 3 & 1 & 3 & -2 \\
 -1 & -2 & 0 & 3 \\
 -3 & -1 & 3 & 3 \\
 2 & -2 & -3 & -3 \\
 0 & 1 & -1 & -2 \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and the vector
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 2.69 \\
 1.68 \\
 2.02 \\
 0.29 \\
 -0.72 \\
 -0.47 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 0.996 \\
 0.239 \\
 -0.008 \\
 0.967 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
