Problem:
Find the least squares vector given the matrix
$\left(
\begin{array}{cccc}
 -1 & 1 & -2 & 0 \\
 1 & 0 & -3 & -2 \\
 3 & -2 & -2 & 1 \\
 2 & 2 & 0 & 1 \\
 0 & -3 & -1 & 0 \\
 0 & 2 & -2 & 1 \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and the vector
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 2.2 \\
 2.28 \\
 2.78 \\
 0.62 \\
 0.71 \\
 1.68 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 0.159 \\
 -0.001 \\
 -0.922 \\
 0.27 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
