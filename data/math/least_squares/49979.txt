Problem:
Find the least squares vector given the matrix
$\left(
\begin{array}{ccc}
 2 & 0 & 2 \\
 -3 & 0 & -2 \\
 2 & 3 & -1 \\
 2 & 2 & 3 \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and the vector
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 1.25 \\
 -2.79 \\
 -1.38 \\
 -0.77 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 0.966 \\
 -1.142 \\
 -0.164 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
