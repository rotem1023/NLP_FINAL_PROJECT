Problem:
Find the least squares vector given the matrix
$\left(
\begin{array}{ccc}
 2 & 1 & 1 \\
 -3 & -1 & -3 \\
 0 & 2 & 2 \\
 1 & -3 & -2 \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and the vector
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -1.19 \\
 2.7 \\
 -2.28 \\
 -1.08 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -0.291 \\
 0.591 \\
 -0.964 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
