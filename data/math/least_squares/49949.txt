Problem:
Find the least squares vector given the matrix
$\left(
\begin{array}{ccc}
 3 & -1 & -1 \\
 -2 & -2 & -3 \\
 -1 & 2 & -2 \\
 2 & 1 & -1 \\
 -3 & -3 & -1 \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and the vector
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -1.87 \\
 2.42 \\
 2.9 \\
 -1.17 \\
 1. \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -0.741 \\
 0.466 \\
 -0.556 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
