Problem:
Find the least squares vector given the matrix
$\left(
\begin{array}{cccc}
 -2 & 1 & -1 & -3 \\
 3 & -3 & 1 & -3 \\
 -1 & -1 & 0 & -3 \\
 3 & 0 & -3 & -2 \\
 2 & 0 & 2 & 3 \\
 1 & 2 & -3 & -2 \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and the vector
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -0.48 \\
 -2.08 \\
 1.06 \\
 -0.74 \\
 0.7 \\
 2.72 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 0.186 \\
 1.112 \\
 0.396 \\
 -0.22 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
