Problem:
Find the least squares vector given the matrix
$\left(
\begin{array}{cc}
 2 & -1 \\
 -1 & 0 \\
 2 & -2 \\
 -1 & -1 \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and the vector
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -1.34 \\
 1.02 \\
 -1.74 \\
 1.89 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -1.136 \\
 -0.459 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
