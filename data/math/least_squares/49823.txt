Problem:
Find the least squares vector given the matrix
$\left(
\begin{array}{ccc}
 3 & 0 & 3 \\
 0 & 0 & 3 \\
 3 & -3 & -1 \\
 3 & -3 & 3 \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and the vector
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 0.99 \\
 2.62 \\
 -1.08 \\
 -0.32 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -0.222 \\
 0.195 \\
 0.552 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
