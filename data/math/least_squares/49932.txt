Problem:
Find the least squares vector given the matrix
$\left(
\begin{array}{cccc}
 2 & 2 & 0 & -2 \\
 2 & 3 & -3 & -3 \\
 3 & 1 & -3 & 2 \\
 -3 & 1 & 2 & 3 \\
 -1 & 1 & 3 & 3 \\
 1 & 0 & 1 & 2 \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and the vector
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 1.41 \\
 1.22 \\
 1.55 \\
 -0.14 \\
 1.21 \\
 2.83 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 0.762 \\
 0.399 \\
 0.408 \\
 0.256 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
