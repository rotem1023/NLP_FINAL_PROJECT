Problem:
Find the least squares vector given the matrix
$\left(
\begin{array}{cc}
 2 & 2 \\
 1 & 2 \\
 1 & -1 \\
 -1 & -2 \\
 -3 & -3 \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and the vector
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 0.13 \\
 1.25 \\
 -2. \\
 -1.03 \\
 2.24 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -1.433 \\
 1.047 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
