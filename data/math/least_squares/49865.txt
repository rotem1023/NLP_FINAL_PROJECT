Problem:
Find the least squares vector given the matrix
$\left(
\begin{array}{cccc}
 3 & -2 & 0 & 2 \\
 0 & 2 & 0 & 3 \\
 -2 & 3 & -1 & 1 \\
 1 & -3 & 0 & 3 \\
 1 & -1 & -3 & 2 \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and the vector
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -2.77 \\
 1.02 \\
 1. \\
 1.05 \\
 -1.4 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -1.34 \\
 -0.366 \\
 0.504 \\
 0.454 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
