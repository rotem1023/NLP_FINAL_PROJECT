Problem:
Find the least squares vector given the matrix
$\left(
\begin{array}{ccccc}
 -3 & 0 & -2 & 1 & -1 \\
 0 & 2 & -1 & -1 & 3 \\
 -3 & -1 & 0 & -2 & 0 \\
 -2 & -3 & 0 & 1 & 1 \\
 1 & -2 & 0 & 0 & -1 \\
 -3 & -3 & 1 & -3 & 0 \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and the vector
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -2.96 \\
 1.91 \\
 1.81 \\
 0.43 \\
 -2.24 \\
 -2.36 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -0.443 \\
 0.733 \\
 1.598 \\
 0.509 \\
 0.902 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
