Problem:
Find the least squares vector given the matrix
$\left(
\begin{array}{ccccc}
 2 & -1 & -1 & 3 & -3 \\
 2 & -1 & 0 & -3 & -2 \\
 -3 & 0 & -1 & 3 & -1 \\
 -1 & -2 & 3 & 2 & -1 \\
 3 & 1 & 3 & -2 & 2 \\
 -3 & -3 & 2 & 2 & 1 \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and the vector
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 0.64 \\
 0.59 \\
 -2.21 \\
 1.9 \\
 0.05 \\
 -2.31 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 0.142 \\
 0.422 \\
 0.477 \\
 -0.112 \\
 -0.626 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
