Problem:
Find the least squares vector given the matrix
$\left(
\begin{array}{ccc}
 -2 & -1 & -3 \\
 -3 & -2 & 0 \\
 2 & 2 & 1 \\
 2 & 3 & 0 \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and the vector
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -0.41 \\
 -1.52 \\
 -0.57 \\
 0. \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 0.984 \\
 -0.761 \\
 -0.341 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
