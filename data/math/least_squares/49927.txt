Problem:
Find the least squares vector given the matrix
$\left(
\begin{array}{cc}
 1 & 2 \\
 0 & 2 \\
 1 & 3 \\
 -1 & -1 \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and the vector
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 2.51 \\
 -0.58 \\
 -1.8 \\
 -0.63 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 1.643 \\
 -0.598 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
