Problem:

\begin{array}{cccc}
 -2 & 1 & 0 & -3 \\
 -2 & 2 & -2 & -3 \\
 1 & 3 & 1 & -2 \\
 -3 & 0 & -2 & 1 \\
 1 & 1 & 0 & 0 \\
 2 & -2 & -2 & -1 \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and the vector
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -0.98 \\
 -2.26 \\
 0.19 \\
 1.87 \\
 2.03 \\
 -2.41 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -0.106 \\
 0.735 \\
 0.031 \\
 0.983 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
