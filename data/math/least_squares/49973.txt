Problem:
Find the least squares vector given the matrix
$\left(
\begin{array}{ccccc}
 0 & -3 & 1 & -2 & -1 \\
 3 & 0 & 3 & 2 & -3 \\
 2 & 1 & -3 & 0 & 1 \\
 1 & 3 & -3 & -1 & 2 \\
 2 & 3 & 2 & -1 & -3 \\
 -2 & 1 & 2 & -3 & 0 \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and the vector
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 0.6 \\
 -2.25 \\
 -0.6 \\
 0.7 \\
 -2.52 \\
 0.36 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -0.091 \\
 -0.237 \\
 0.132 \\
 -0.139 \\
 0.693 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
