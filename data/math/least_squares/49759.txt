Problem:
Find the least squares vector given the matrix
$\left(
\begin{array}{cccc}
 0 & -1 & 2 & -2 \\
 -3 & 1 & 3 & -2 \\
 3 & -2 & -3 & 1 \\
 1 & -1 & 1 & 0 \\
 -3 & -1 & 0 & 2 \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and the vector
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -2.52 \\
 0.61 \\
 -1.38 \\
 1.6 \\
 -0.02 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 0.686 \\
 0.991 \\
 1.255 \\
 1.586 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
