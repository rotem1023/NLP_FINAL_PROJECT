Problem:
Find the least squares vector given the matrix
$\left(
\begin{array}{ccccc}
 -2 & -2 & 0 & 1 & 0 \\
 0 & 2 & 2 & 0 & -1 \\
 2 & 3 & -1 & 3 & 1 \\
 -2 & -1 & 3 & 2 & 2 \\
 -2 & -3 & 3 & -2 & -1 \\
 1 & 2 & -2 & -3 & -1 \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and the vector
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 2.61 \\
 2.75 \\
 -0.22 \\
 2.84 \\
 -0.41 \\
 0.66 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -2.79 \\
 1.577 \\
 -0.348 \\
 0.208 \\
 -0.266 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
