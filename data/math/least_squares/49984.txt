Problem:
Find the least squares vector given the matrix
$\left(
\begin{array}{ccccc}
 3 & 2 & -3 & -3 & 3 \\
 -3 & -2 & 2 & 1 & 0 \\
 3 & -3 & 3 & 0 & 1 \\
 1 & 0 & 2 & 3 & 1 \\
 3 & -2 & 3 & 3 & 0 \\
 2 & 3 & -2 & -1 & 1 \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and the vector
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 2.13 \\
 -2.02 \\
 2.57 \\
 -1.02 \\
 -0.61 \\
 0.2 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 0.449 \\
 -0.019 \\
 0.383 \\
 -0.86 \\
 -0.184 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
