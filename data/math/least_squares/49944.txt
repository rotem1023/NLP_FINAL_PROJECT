Problem:
Find the least squares vector given the matrix
$\left(
\begin{array}{ccccc}
 -3 & -2 & -1 & 3 & -1 \\
 -1 & 0 & 3 & -3 & 0 \\
 2 & 0 & 0 & 3 & 1 \\
 -2 & -1 & 3 & -3 & -2 \\
 3 & -3 & -3 & -1 & -3 \\
 0 & -2 & 0 & 1 & -1 \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and the vector
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 2.54 \\
 -0.62 \\
 -2.61 \\
 1.81 \\
 1.33 \\
 -2.07 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -0.718 \\
 1.457 \\
 -0.586 \\
 0.072 \\
 -1.923 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
