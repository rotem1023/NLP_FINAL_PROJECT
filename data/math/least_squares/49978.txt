Problem:
Find the least squares vector given the matrix
$\left(
\begin{array}{ccc}
 -2 & -2 & 3 \\
 2 & -1 & -3 \\
 -2 & 2 & -3 \\
 -2 & 0 & -3 \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and the vector
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -0.39 \\
 -2.07 \\
 1.56 \\
 -2.23 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 0.018 \\
 1.151 \\
 0.484 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
