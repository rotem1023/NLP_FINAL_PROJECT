Problem:
Find the least squares vector given the matrix
$\left(
\begin{array}{cc}
 3 & -3 \\
 1 & -3 \\
 3 & 0 \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and the vector
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -0.23 \\
 -2.53 \\
 -1.37 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -0.165 \\
 0.35 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
