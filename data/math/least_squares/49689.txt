Problem:
Find the least squares vector given the matrix
$\left(
\begin{array}{cc}
 1 & -1 \\
 -1 & -2 \\
 3 & 1 \\
 -3 & 2 \\
 1 & -2 \\
 -1 & 1 \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and the vector
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -0.18 \\
 -0.51 \\
 2.55 \\
 -1.05 \\
 2.88 \\
 -1.36 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 0.666 \\
 -0.143 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
