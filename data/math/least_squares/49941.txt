Problem:
Find the least squares vector given the matrix
$\left(
\begin{array}{ccccc}
 -3 & 3 & -1 & -3 & 2 \\
 1 & 3 & -2 & -2 & -3 \\
 -1 & -3 & -1 & -2 & 0 \\
 -3 & 3 & 2 & 3 & 2 \\
 0 & -3 & -1 & -3 & -3 \\
 0 & -2 & 0 & 3 & 3 \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and the vector
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 2.61 \\
 -1.61 \\
 1.1 \\
 1.16 \\
 2.83 \\
 -1.83 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -0.766 \\
 -0.218 \\
 0.994 \\
 -0.688 \\
 -0.177 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
