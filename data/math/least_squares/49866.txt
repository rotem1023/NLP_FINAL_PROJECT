Problem:
Find the least squares vector given the matrix
$\left(
\begin{array}{cccc}
 1 & 0 & 1 & 3 \\
 -3 & 3 & -2 & 1 \\
 3 & 0 & 0 & 2 \\
 0 & 2 & 3 & 0 \\
 -2 & -1 & 3 & -2 \\
 1 & 0 & -1 & -3 \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and the vector
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -0.72 \\
 -0.63 \\
 -0.47 \\
 -0.68 \\
 0.83 \\
 -0.77 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -0.241 \\
 -0.416 \\
 0.024 \\
 0.027 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
