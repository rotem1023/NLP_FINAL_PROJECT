Problem:
Find the least squares vector given the matrix
$\left(
\begin{array}{cc}
 -1 & 0 \\
 -1 & 2 \\
 2 & 2 \\
 2 & 3 \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and the vector
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -1.51 \\
 0.18 \\
 0.93 \\
 -2.6 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 0.099 \\
 -0.375 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
