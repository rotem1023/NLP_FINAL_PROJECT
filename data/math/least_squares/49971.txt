Problem:
Find the least squares vector given the matrix
$\left(
\begin{array}{ccc}
 1 & 3 & 0 \\
 -1 & 1 & 0 \\
 -3 & 0 & 0 \\
 2 & -1 & -1 \\
 -1 & 1 & 2 \\
 2 & 1 & 2 \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and the vector
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 1.18 \\
 -1.15 \\
 2.14 \\
 -2.96 \\
 1.94 \\
 -2.6 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -0.88 \\
 0.456 \\
 -0.071 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
