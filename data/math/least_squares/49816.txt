Problem:
Find the least squares vector given the matrix
$\left(
\begin{array}{ccc}
 -1 & -3 & -3 \\
 0 & -2 & 3 \\
 2 & 3 & 3 \\
 3 & -3 & 3 \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and the vector
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -2.27 \\
 2.09 \\
 -2.54 \\
 2.73 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -0.435 \\
 -0.487 \\
 0.637 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
