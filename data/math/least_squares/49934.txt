Problem:
Find the least squares vector given the matrix
$\left(
\begin{array}{ccccc}
 1 & 3 & 3 & 0 & 2 \\
 0 & -1 & 3 & -3 & 2 \\
 3 & -3 & 3 & 2 & -1 \\
 1 & 3 & -1 & -3 & -3 \\
 -3 & 2 & 2 & -1 & -3 \\
 -1 & 3 & 0 & 2 & 1 \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and the vector
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -2.11 \\
 -0.22 \\
 -0.83 \\
 -1.13 \\
 2.21 \\
 -2.31 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -0.677 \\
 -0.495 \\
 -0.002 \\
 -0.145 \\
 -0.295 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
