Problem:
Find the least squares vector given the matrix
$\left(
\begin{array}{cccc}
 0 & 0 & -3 & 3 \\
 3 & -1 & 3 & 0 \\
 -2 & 2 & -2 & -2 \\
 -3 & 2 & 2 & 3 \\
 1 & -3 & 3 & 2 \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and the vector
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 1.07 \\
 1.62 \\
 -0.8 \\
 0.2 \\
 -2.54 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 1.129 \\
 1.311 \\
 -0.058 \\
 0.36 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
