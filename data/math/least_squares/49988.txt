Problem:
Find the least squares vector given the matrix
$\left(
\begin{array}{ccc}
 -3 & 2 & 1 \\
 3 & -1 & 3 \\
 3 & -1 & 3 \\
 0 & 0 & -1 \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and the vector
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 1. \\
 2.73 \\
 -1.01 \\
 0.4 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 1.84 \\
 3.46 \\
 -0.4 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
