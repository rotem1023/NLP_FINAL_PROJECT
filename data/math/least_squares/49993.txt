Problem:
Find the least squares vector given the matrix
$\left(
\begin{array}{ccc}
 -2 & 1 & -1 \\
 2 & -1 & 2 \\
 -3 & -2 & 2 \\
 -2 & -2 & 1 \\
 -1 & 2 & -3 \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and the vector
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -1.17 \\
 -2.79 \\
 -1.1 \\
 -2.72 \\
 1.9 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 0.154 \\
 0.212 \\
 -0.632 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
