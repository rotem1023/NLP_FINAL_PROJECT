Problem:
Find the least squares vector given the matrix
$\left(
\begin{array}{ccccc}
 0 & -2 & 1 & 3 & 2 \\
 2 & 2 & 3 & 3 & 3 \\
 -3 & -2 & 3 & -3 & 2 \\
 3 & 1 & 2 & -2 & 3 \\
 -2 & 3 & 2 & 2 & 3 \\
 -3 & 0 & 3 & -2 & 1 \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and the vector
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -1.25 \\
 -1.47 \\
 1.46 \\
 -1.28 \\
 -2.8 \\
 -1.46 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 0.106 \\
 -0.379 \\
 0.014 \\
 -0.191 \\
 -0.361 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
