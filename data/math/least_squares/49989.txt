Problem:
Find the least squares vector given the matrix
$\left(
\begin{array}{ccc}
 -1 & 3 & -2 \\
 0 & 3 & 0 \\
 2 & 1 & 1 \\
 0 & -3 & -3 \\
 0 & 3 & -1 \\
 -2 & 1 & 3 \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and the vector
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 0.67 \\
 -2.26 \\
 1.01 \\
 2.89 \\
 -1.45 \\
 1.12 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -0.267 \\
 -0.42 \\
 -0.127 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
