Problem:
Find the least squares vector given the matrix
$\left(
\begin{array}{ccc}
 3 & -1 & -1 \\
 1 & 0 & -2 \\
 0 & -2 & 3 \\
 -2 & -1 & 3 \\
 -2 & -2 & 1 \\
 -3 & 1 & -1 \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and the vector
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 0.32 \\
 0.06 \\
 -2.15 \\
 2.54 \\
 -0.28 \\
 -2.84 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 0.341 \\
 0.342 \\
 0.419 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
