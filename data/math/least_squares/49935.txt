Problem:
Find the least squares vector given the matrix
$\left(
\begin{array}{cc}
 -1 & -2 \\
 -1 & 3 \\
 0 & 2 \\
 3 & 2 \\
 1 & 1 \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and the vector
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 1.55 \\
 -1.32 \\
 -0.91 \\
 2.31 \\
 -2.25 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 0.601 \\
 -0.46 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
