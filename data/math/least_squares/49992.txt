Problem:
Find the least squares vector given the matrix
$\left(
\begin{array}{ccc}
 3 & 2 & 1 \\
 -1 & 3 & 2 \\
 3 & -3 & 3 \\
 -3 & -1 & 1 \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and the vector
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -0.9 \\
 2.25 \\
 0.2 \\
 -2.68 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 0.148 \\
 0.332 \\
 0.076 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
