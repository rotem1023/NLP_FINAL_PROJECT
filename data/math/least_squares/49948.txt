Problem:
Find the least squares vector given the matrix
$\left(
\begin{array}{ccc}
 3 & -1 & 1 \\
 1 & -3 & -3 \\
 3 & 1 & -2 \\
 1 & 0 & 3 \\
 3 & 1 & 2 \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and the vector
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -0.4 \\
 -2.02 \\
 2.55 \\
 -2.29 \\
 -1.26 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 0.01 \\
 1.077 \\
 -0.647 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
