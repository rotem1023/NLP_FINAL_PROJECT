Problem:
Find the least squares vector given the matrix
$\left(
\begin{array}{ccc}
 -1 & -1 & -3 \\
 1 & 0 & 0 \\
 3 & 2 & 1 \\
 -2 & -3 & 2 \\
 3 & -1 & 0 \\
 2 & 0 & -3 \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and the vector
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 2.13 \\
 -0.19 \\
 -1.28 \\
 0.71 \\
 -1.38 \\
 0.96 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -0.362 \\
 -0.152 \\
 -0.467 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
