Problem:
Find the least squares vector given the matrix
$\left(
\begin{array}{cccc}
 0 & -1 & 2 & -3 \\
 2 & -3 & -3 & 3 \\
 2 & 3 & 3 & 0 \\
 2 & -2 & -3 & 1 \\
 -3 & 3 & -3 & -1 \\
 2 & 2 & -3 & -1 \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and the vector
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 1.9 \\
 -1.48 \\
 -0.64 \\
 -0.62 \\
 -2.04 \\
 -0.59 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 0.053 \\
 -0.364 \\
 0.265 \\
 -0.484 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
