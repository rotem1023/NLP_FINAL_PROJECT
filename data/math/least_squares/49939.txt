Problem:
Find the least squares vector given the matrix
$\left(
\begin{array}{ccccc}
 3 & 2 & 2 & -3 & 2 \\
 -1 & 1 & -1 & -3 & 3 \\
 1 & 1 & 3 & 3 & -1 \\
 3 & -3 & -3 & 0 & -2 \\
 0 & 1 & 2 & 2 & 1 \\
 -3 & 1 & -3 & -2 & 3 \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and the vector
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 2.33 \\
 0.15 \\
 0.17 \\
 -2.52 \\
 -2.91 \\
 2.63 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 0.105 \\
 4.382 \\
 -1.703 \\
 -0.624 \\
 -2.594 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
