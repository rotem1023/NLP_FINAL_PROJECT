Problem:
Find the least squares vector given the matrix
$\left(
\begin{array}{ccc}
 1 & -2 & 2 \\
 3 & 2 & 2 \\
 2 & 1 & 0 \\
 3 & -1 & -2 \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and the vector
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 1.43 \\
 1.17 \\
 0.73 \\
 0.1 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 0.277 \\
 -0.151 \\
 0.396 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
