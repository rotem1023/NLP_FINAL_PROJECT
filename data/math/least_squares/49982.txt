Problem:
Find the least squares vector given the matrix
$\left(
\begin{array}{cc}
 2 & -1 \\
 -3 & -2 \\
 -1 & 2 \\
 3 & 2 \\
 0 & 3 \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and the vector
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -0.21 \\
 2.93 \\
 1.26 \\
 2.07 \\
 -0.53 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -0.202 \\
 0.047 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
