Problem:
Find the least squares vector given the matrix
$\left(
\begin{array}{cccc}
 -2 & -3 & 2 & 3 \\
 -3 & 0 & -1 & 0 \\
 -2 & 0 & 3 & 3 \\
 -1 & -3 & 1 & -3 \\
 -3 & 2 & 0 & -1 \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and the vector
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 0.28 \\
 2.47 \\
 0.82 \\
 -0.44 \\
 -0.2 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -0.39 \\
 -0.128 \\
 -0.519 \\
 0.302 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
