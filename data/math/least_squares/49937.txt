Problem:
Find the least squares vector given the matrix
$\left(
\begin{array}{cccc}
 2 & -2 & -2 & 3 \\
 -2 & -1 & -3 & -1 \\
 1 & 0 & -3 & 2 \\
 3 & -1 & 1 & -3 \\
 2 & -3 & 1 & -2 \\
 1 & -2 & 1 & 3 \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and the vector
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -0.88 \\
 0.9 \\
 -0.92 \\
 2.43 \\
 -0.33 \\
 0.73 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 0.054 \\
 -0.101 \\
 0.066 \\
 -0.258 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
