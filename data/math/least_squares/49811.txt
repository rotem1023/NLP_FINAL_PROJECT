Problem:
Find the least squares vector given the matrix
$\left(
\begin{array}{ccc}
 -3 & -3 & 1 \\
 -1 & -2 & 0 \\
 0 & -3 & 1 \\
 2 & -3 & -2 \\
 3 & 2 & 0 \\
 -3 & 1 & 0 \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and the vector
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 2.11 \\
 -0.13 \\
 -0.07 \\
 2.36 \\
 -0.36 \\
 0.89 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -0.25 \\
 -0.299 \\
 -0.739 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
