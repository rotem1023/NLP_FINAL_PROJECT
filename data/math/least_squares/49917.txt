Problem:
Find the least squares vector given the matrix
$\left(
\begin{array}{cccc}
 3 & 0 & -3 & 1 \\
 2 & -1 & 0 & 3 \\
 -2 & -3 & -2 & 1 \\
 -1 & -1 & -1 & -2 \\
 1 & 0 & -3 & 1 \\
 0 & 0 & 2 & -2 \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and the vector
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 1.98 \\
 1.37 \\
 -2.48 \\
 1.36 \\
 -2.05 \\
 2.24 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 1.512 \\
 -0.986 \\
 0.61 \\
 -0.93 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
