Problem:
Find the least squares vector given the matrix
$\left(
\begin{array}{ccccc}
 0 & 3 & -3 & 0 & 2 \\
 2 & 2 & -3 & -2 & 3 \\
 -3 & 1 & 0 & 0 & 2 \\
 3 & -3 & -2 & -1 & -2 \\
 2 & 3 & 2 & -1 & 2 \\
 3 & 3 & 0 & -3 & -2 \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and the vector
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -0.16 \\
 0.93 \\
 2.98 \\
 -2.38 \\
 0.11 \\
 1.54 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -0.969 \\
 0.098 \\
 0.16 \\
 -1.422 \\
 0.06 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
