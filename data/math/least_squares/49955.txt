Problem:
Find the least squares vector given the matrix
$\left(
\begin{array}{ccccc}
 0 & -3 & 0 & 3 & 2 \\
 1 & -2 & 0 & -1 & 1 \\
 3 & 3 & -1 & 3 & -2 \\
 3 & -3 & 1 & 1 & -1 \\
 -2 & -3 & 1 & 1 & -3 \\
 1 & -1 & 0 & 1 & -1 \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and the vector
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 2.98 \\
 -0.63 \\
 -2.02 \\
 1.5 \\
 -1.09 \\
 -0.46 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 0.042 \\
 0.468 \\
 3.24 \\
 0.704 \\
 1.145 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
