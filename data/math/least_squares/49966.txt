Problem:
Find the least squares vector given the matrix
$\left(
\begin{array}{ccccc}
 1 & 3 & -2 & -3 & 1 \\
 3 & -3 & -3 & -3 & 0 \\
 0 & 1 & 2 & -2 & 2 \\
 1 & -2 & -1 & 0 & 3 \\
 -2 & 2 & 0 & 3 & 0 \\
 -3 & 3 & 3 & -2 & -3 \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and the vector
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -1.76 \\
 1.26 \\
 1.53 \\
 2.02 \\
 -1.59 \\
 2.21 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -1.832 \\
 -1.173 \\
 -0.101 \\
 -0.975 \\
 0.471 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
