Problem:
Find the least squares vector given the matrix
$\left(
\begin{array}{cc}
 2 & 0 \\
 -1 & 2 \\
 -3 & -1 \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and the vector
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -0.71 \\
 -1.07 \\
 -2.64 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 0.541 \\
 -0.008 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
