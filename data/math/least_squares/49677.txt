Problem:
Find the least squares vector given the matrix
$\left(
\begin{array}{ccc}
 3 & 1 & -3 \\
 -1 & -3 & 0 \\
 1 & -1 & 2 \\
 1 & 1 & -1 \\
 -1 & -2 & -3 \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and the vector
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -1.96 \\
 1.54 \\
 -1.64 \\
 2.65 \\
 1.23 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -0.768 \\
 0.087 \\
 -0.33 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
