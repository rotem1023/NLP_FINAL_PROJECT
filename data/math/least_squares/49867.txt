Problem:
Find the least squares vector given the matrix
$\left(
\begin{array}{ccc}
 -2 & 2 & 1 \\
 3 & 1 & 0 \\
 1 & -1 & -2 \\
 0 & 2 & 2 \\
 -2 & -1 & -2 \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and the vector
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 2.49 \\
 -2.56 \\
 1.84 \\
 -1.44 \\
 -0.15 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -0.584 \\
 0.227 \\
 -0.464 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
