Problem:
Find the least squares vector given the matrix
$\left(
\begin{array}{ccc}
 0 & -1 & -3 \\
 1 & 1 & 1 \\
 -3 & -2 & 1 \\
 3 & -3 & 3 \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and the vector
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -2.41 \\
 1.84 \\
 -0.87 \\
 2.46 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 0.377 \\
 0.31 \\
 0.756 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
