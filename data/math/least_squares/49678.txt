Problem:
Find the least squares vector given the matrix
$\left(
\begin{array}{ccc}
 2 & 3 & -2 \\
 0 & 1 & -1 \\
 -3 & -1 & -1 \\
 3 & 3 & -3 \\
 0 & -3 & 0 \\
 -1 & -3 & -2 \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and the vector
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -0.74 \\
 0.3 \\
 -2.43 \\
 2.08 \\
 -0.74 \\
 -1.85 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 0.616 \\
 0.131 \\
 0.378 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
