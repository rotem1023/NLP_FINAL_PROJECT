Problem:
Find the least squares vector given the matrix
$\left(
\begin{array}{cccc}
 2 & -1 & 0 & -1 \\
 1 & 0 & 1 & 1 \\
 -3 & 2 & 2 & 1 \\
 1 & 2 & -1 & 0 \\
 -3 & 2 & 2 & 3 \\
 0 & -1 & 0 & -1 \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and the vector
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -1.89 \\
 1.89 \\
 0.31 \\
 -0.82 \\
 0.67 \\
 -2.1 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 0.1 \\
 -0.324 \\
 -0.267 \\
 1.165 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
