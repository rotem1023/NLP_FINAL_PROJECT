Problem:
Find the least squares vector given the matrix
$\left(
\begin{array}{cc}
 -3 & -3 \\
 0 & -1 \\
 -1 & -3 \\
 -3 & 2 \\
 -3 & 2 \\
 1 & 3 \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and the vector
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -1.67 \\
 0.96 \\
 1.34 \\
 -2.65 \\
 2.87 \\
 1.44 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 0.141 \\
 0.121 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
