Problem:
Find the least squares vector given the matrix
$\left(
\begin{array}{cccc}
 -2 & 3 & -2 & -1 \\
 0 & 1 & 2 & 3 \\
 3 & 1 & 0 & -1 \\
 -2 & 0 & -3 & 2 \\
 3 & 0 & 1 & 2 \\
 0 & 0 & -2 & -2 \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and the vector
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -0.19 \\
 -2.72 \\
 -2.91 \\
 0.99 \\
 -0.8 \\
 -0.19 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -0.476 \\
 -0.777 \\
 -0.196 \\
 -0.132 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
