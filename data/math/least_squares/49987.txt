Problem:
Find the least squares vector given the matrix
$\left(
\begin{array}{ccc}
 3 & 2 & -3 \\
 -3 & 3 & -2 \\
 2 & 1 & 2 \\
 2 & 3 & 0 \\
 3 & 1 & 1 \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and the vector
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -1.87 \\
 -2.96 \\
 -2.68 \\
 -2.04 \\
 -1.57 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -0.045 \\
 -1.038 \\
 -0.254 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
