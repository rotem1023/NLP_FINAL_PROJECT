Problem:
Find the least squares vector given the matrix
$\left(
\begin{array}{cccc}
 -2 & 3 & 2 & 1 \\
 2 & 3 & 3 & -2 \\
 -2 & -3 & 2 & 3 \\
 -2 & 0 & -2 & -2 \\
 0 & 3 & -3 & 1 \\
 0 & -2 & 2 & -3 \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and the vector
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -2.66 \\
 0.3 \\
 -0.17 \\
 -2.45 \\
 -1.94 \\
 -0.64 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 0.981 \\
 -0.398 \\
 0.058 \\
 0.284 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
