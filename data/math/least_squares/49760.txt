Problem:
Find the least squares vector given the matrix
$\left(
\begin{array}{cc}
 0 & -1 \\
 2 & 3 \\
 3 & -1 \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and the vector
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -2.26 \\
 -1.7 \\
 -1.42 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -0.597 \\
 0.034 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
