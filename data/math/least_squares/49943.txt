Problem:
Find the least squares vector given the matrix
$\left(
\begin{array}{cccc}
 -2 & -3 & -3 & 3 \\
 0 & -1 & 1 & 0 \\
 -2 & 3 & -3 & -3 \\
 2 & -2 & 1 & -1 \\
 0 & -1 & 2 & 0 \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and the vector
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 1.87 \\
 -0.48 \\
 -0.28 \\
 2.36 \\
 -2.88 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 1.666 \\
 -0.286 \\
 -1.388 \\
 0.077 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
