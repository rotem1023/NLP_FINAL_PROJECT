Problem:
Find the least squares vector given the matrix
$\left(
\begin{array}{ccc}
 -3 & -2 & 2 \\
 0 & 3 & 0 \\
 2 & -2 & 1 \\
 0 & 0 & -3 \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and the vector
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -1.01 \\
 -1.26 \\
 -0.3 \\
 -1.44 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 0.254 \\
 -0.026 \\
 0.204 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
