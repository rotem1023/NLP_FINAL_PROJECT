Problem:
Find the least squares vector given the matrix
$\left(
\begin{array}{ccc}
 0 & -3 & -3 \\
 2 & 3 & -3 \\
 2 & 0 & -1 \\
 0 & 2 & -2 \\
 1 & -2 & 2 \\
 3 & -3 & 1 \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and the vector
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -0.05 \\
 -0.86 \\
 1.77 \\
 -0.62 \\
 2.06 \\
 -1.44 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -0.014 \\
 -0.053 \\
 0.152 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
