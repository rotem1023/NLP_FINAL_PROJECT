Problem:
Find the least squares vector given the matrix
$\left(
\begin{array}{ccc}
 0 & 0 & 0 \\
 -3 & 3 & -1 \\
 0 & -1 & 1 \\
 -3 & 2 & 1 \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and the vector
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 2.89 \\
 -1.95 \\
 2.05 \\
 1.55 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -0.433 \\
 -0.6 \\
 1.45 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
