Problem:
Find the least squares vector given the matrix
$\left(
\begin{array}{cccc}
 1 & 3 & -2 & -2 \\
 -3 & 3 & 0 & -3 \\
 -3 & 2 & 2 & -3 \\
 3 & -1 & -2 & 3 \\
 3 & -2 & 0 & 2 \\
 2 & 3 & 3 & 1 \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and the vector
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 2.7 \\
 -2.49 \\
 2.41 \\
 -2.2 \\
 0.62 \\
 1.74 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 1.656 \\
 -0.656 \\
 0.843 \\
 -2.101 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
