Problem:
Find the least squares vector given the matrix
$\left(
\begin{array}{ccc}
 -2 & 2 & 0 \\
 2 & -1 & 3 \\
 0 & -3 & -3 \\
 -3 & 3 & 0 \\
 1 & 3 & 1 \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and the vector
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 2.96 \\
 1.98 \\
 -1.34 \\
 2.37 \\
 -2.75 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -1.809 \\
 -0.876 \\
 1.461 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
