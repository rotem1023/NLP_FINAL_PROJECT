Problem:
Find the least squares vector given the matrix
$\left(
\begin{array}{cccc}
 -3 & -2 & -2 & -3 \\
 1 & 2 & 2 & -3 \\
 2 & 1 & -1 & 0 \\
 -3 & -2 & -3 & -2 \\
 2 & 0 & -2 & 1 \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and the vector
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -1.68 \\
 1.94 \\
 0.48 \\
 -0.72 \\
 -0.29 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -0.73 \\
 1.912 \\
 -0.368 \\
 0.192 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
