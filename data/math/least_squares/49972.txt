Problem:
Find the least squares vector given the matrix
$\left(
\begin{array}{cccc}
 -1 & 0 & 1 & 1 \\
 3 & -1 & 2 & 1 \\
 -3 & 3 & 2 & 1 \\
 -3 & -3 & -2 & 3 \\
 -3 & -3 & -2 & 1 \\
 0 & -1 & -2 & 0 \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and the vector
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -0.24 \\
 0.66 \\
 -2.45 \\
 -0.36 \\
 -2.85 \\
 -1.17 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 0.711 \\
 0.292 \\
 -0.33 \\
 0.401 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
