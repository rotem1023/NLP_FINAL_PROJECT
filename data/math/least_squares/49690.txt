Problem:
Find the least squares vector given the matrix
$\left(
\begin{array}{cc}
 0 & 1 \\
 -1 & -1 \\
 -3 & 3 \\
 0 & 3 \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and the vector
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -0.97 \\
 -2.8 \\
 2.25 \\
 -0.05 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -0.085 \\
 0.388 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
