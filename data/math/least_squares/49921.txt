Problem:
Find the least squares vector given the matrix
$\left(
\begin{array}{cc}
 -3 & 0 \\
 0 & -1 \\
 -3 & 3 \\
 -1 & -2 \\
 0 & 3 \\
 -1 & 3 \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and the vector
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 1.92 \\
 2.96 \\
 2.47 \\
 -2.07 \\
 2.22 \\
 0.64 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -0.378 \\
 0.419 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
