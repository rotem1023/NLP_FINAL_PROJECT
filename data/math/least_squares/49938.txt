Problem:
Find the least squares vector given the matrix
$\left(
\begin{array}{ccccc}
 -1 & -3 & -3 & -2 & 1 \\
 -2 & -2 & 0 & -1 & -3 \\
 -3 & -2 & 2 & 0 & 3 \\
 0 & 3 & -2 & -1 & -2 \\
 2 & -3 & 3 & 2 & 3 \\
 -3 & -1 & 1 & 1 & -3 \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and the vector
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -2.3 \\
 -0.9 \\
 2.58 \\
 -2.21 \\
 1.76 \\
 -2.12 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 0.218 \\
 0.28 \\
 1.399 \\
 -1.338 \\
 0.387 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
