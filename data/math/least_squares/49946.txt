Problem:
Find the least squares vector given the matrix
$\left(
\begin{array}{ccc}
 -1 & 3 & 3 \\
 0 & 2 & -2 \\
 1 & -1 & -3 \\
 -1 & 2 & -2 \\
 -2 & 3 & -3 \\
 -2 & 2 & -1 \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and the vector
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -2.17 \\
 0.1 \\
 -1.17 \\
 -1.58 \\
 0.81 \\
 -1.93 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -0.063 \\
 -0.364 \\
 -0.079 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
