Problem:
Find the least squares vector given the matrix
$\left(
\begin{array}{cc}
 -3 & 3 \\
 -3 & -3 \\
 2 & -1 \\
 3 & -1 \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and the vector
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -1.25 \\
 -2.94 \\
 1.18 \\
 -0.63 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 0.476 \\
 0.345 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
