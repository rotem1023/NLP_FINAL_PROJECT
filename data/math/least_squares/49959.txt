Problem:
Find the least squares vector given the matrix
$\left(
\begin{array}{ccccc}
 0 & 2 & 2 & 1 & 1 \\
 2 & 0 & -2 & -1 & 3 \\
 -3 & -1 & -1 & 3 & 1 \\
 1 & -3 & -3 & 1 & -1 \\
 1 & -3 & -1 & -2 & -2 \\
 0 & -2 & -2 & 2 & 1 \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and the vector
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -2.82 \\
 0.71 \\
 2.81 \\
 -0.13 \\
 0.85 \\
 -1. \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -1.82 \\
 0.414 \\
 -1.354 \\
 -1.29 \\
 0.075 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
