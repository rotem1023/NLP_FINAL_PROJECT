Problem:
Find the least squares vector given the matrix
$\left(
\begin{array}{ccccc}
 -1 & 0 & -2 & 1 & 1 \\
 -1 & -1 & 1 & 0 & 1 \\
 0 & 1 & -2 & 3 & 1 \\
 -1 & -1 & 0 & -1 & 0 \\
 3 & 3 & -2 & 3 & 3 \\
 0 & -3 & 2 & -3 & 3 \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and the vector
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -0.98 \\
 0.7 \\
 1.05 \\
 0.65 \\
 1.68 \\
 -1.55 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -0.597 \\
 0.965 \\
 0.766 \\
 0.327 \\
 0.302 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
