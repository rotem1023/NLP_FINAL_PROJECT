Problem:
Find the least squares vector given the matrix
$\left(
\begin{array}{ccccc}
 -1 & 3 & -2 & 3 & 1 \\
 3 & -2 & 3 & -2 & -2 \\
 -3 & 3 & -2 & -2 & 2 \\
 3 & -2 & 2 & -2 & 3 \\
 -1 & 2 & -3 & -1 & -1 \\
 2 & -1 & -3 & -2 & -3 \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and the vector
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 0.5 \\
 -1.59 \\
 -0.05 \\
 1.66 \\
 -0.6 \\
 1.43 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 0.198 \\
 -0.631 \\
 -0.701 \\
 0.163 \\
 0.545 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
