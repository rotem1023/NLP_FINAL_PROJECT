Problem:
Find the least squares vector given the matrix
$\left(
\begin{array}{ccc}
 -3 & -2 & -2 \\
 2 & 3 & 3 \\
 3 & -2 & 3 \\
 3 & -2 & -3 \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and the vector
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -2.64 \\
 1.91 \\
 1.12 \\
 1.33 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 0.633 \\
 0.318 \\
 -0.043 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
