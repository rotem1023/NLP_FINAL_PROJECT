Problem:
Find the least squares vector given the matrix
$\left(
\begin{array}{ccccc}
 3 & 3 & 1 & -3 & 1 \\
 2 & 1 & 3 & 3 & -1 \\
 3 & -2 & 1 & 3 & 3 \\
 2 & -1 & 2 & -1 & 3 \\
 -3 & -1 & 3 & -2 & 3 \\
 1 & -1 & 2 & 0 & 0 \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and the vector
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -2.72 \\
 -0.73 \\
 0.14 \\
 -2.51 \\
 0.72 \\
 1.38 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -0.286 \\
 -0.634 \\
 0.092 \\
 0.061 \\
 -0.427 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
