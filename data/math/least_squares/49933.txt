Problem:
Find the least squares vector given the matrix
$\left(
\begin{array}{ccccc}
 0 & 2 & 0 & 3 & 2 \\
 2 & 0 & 3 & 0 & -1 \\
 0 & -2 & -3 & -3 & -2 \\
 -3 & 2 & -1 & -3 & 0 \\
 -1 & -3 & 0 & 0 & 3 \\
 1 & 1 & 1 & 1 & 1 \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and the vector
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -1.26 \\
 1.22 \\
 -2. \\
 -1.41 \\
 -1.06 \\
 2.85 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 0.974 \\
 0.378 \\
 0.333 \\
 -0.478 \\
 0.458 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
