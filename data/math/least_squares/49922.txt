Problem:
Find the least squares vector given the matrix
$\left(
\begin{array}{cccc}
 1 & -1 & -2 & -2 \\
 3 & -3 & 0 & -1 \\
 0 & -2 & 3 & -2 \\
 1 & 0 & -2 & -1 \\
 -1 & 0 & 2 & 2 \\
 2 & 3 & 0 & 3 \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and the vector
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -0.55 \\
 1.54 \\
 -0.19 \\
 -0.59 \\
 -1.49 \\
 1.88 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 0.685 \\
 0.287 \\
 0.202 \\
 -0.062 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
