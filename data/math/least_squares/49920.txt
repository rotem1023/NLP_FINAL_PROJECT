Problem:
Find the least squares vector given the matrix
$\left(
\begin{array}{cc}
 -3 & -3 \\
 2 & -1 \\
 3 & -2 \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and the vector
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -0.63 \\
 0.68 \\
 2.99 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 0.573 \\
 -0.382 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
