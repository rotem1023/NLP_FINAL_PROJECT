Problem:
Find the least squares vector given the matrix
$\left(
\begin{array}{cccc}
 -3 & -3 & 3 & -2 \\
 2 & -2 & -3 & -3 \\
 3 & 3 & -3 & 2 \\
 3 & -1 & -1 & 3 \\
 1 & 3 & -3 & -1 \\
 2 & 3 & -2 & -3 \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and the vector
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 2.55 \\
 1.37 \\
 -2.9 \\
 1.81 \\
 -1.04 \\
 0.35 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 0.813 \\
 -0.459 \\
 0.731 \\
 -0.47 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
