Problem:
Find the least squares vector given the matrix
$\left(
\begin{array}{cc}
 3 & 2 \\
 -3 & 2 \\
 -3 & -2 \\
 3 & 2 \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and the vector
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 2.46 \\
 -2.39 \\
 2.18 \\
 -0.12 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 0.407 \\
 -0.584 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
