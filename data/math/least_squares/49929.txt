Problem:
Find the least squares vector given the matrix
$\left(
\begin{array}{cccc}
 1 & 1 & -1 & 3 \\
 0 & -3 & -2 & -1 \\
 2 & -3 & 0 & -2 \\
 -1 & -2 & -1 & 3 \\
 0 & 0 & 3 & 3 \\
 1 & 3 & -1 & -2 \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and the vector
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 1.54 \\
 2.39 \\
 0.78 \\
 1.15 \\
 -0.98 \\
 -0.43 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 0.328 \\
 -0.282 \\
 -0.636 \\
 0.235 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
