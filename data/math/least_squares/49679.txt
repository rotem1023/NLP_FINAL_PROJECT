Problem:
Find the least squares vector given the matrix
$\left(
\begin{array}{cccc}
 -2 & 1 & 2 & -2 \\
 2 & 0 & -3 & -3 \\
 -3 & 0 & -1 & 0 \\
 2 & 2 & 0 & 2 \\
 -1 & 2 & -2 & 3 \\
 -1 & 2 & 3 & -3 \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and the vector
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 2.75 \\
 -1.01 \\
 1.44 \\
 1.63 \\
 -2.76 \\
 1.39 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -0.113 \\
 0.109 \\
 0.502 \\
 -0.19 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
