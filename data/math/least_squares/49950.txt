Problem:
Find the least squares vector given the matrix
$\left(
\begin{array}{cc}
 0 & 2 \\
 -2 & 3 \\
 2 & 3 \\
 -2 & 3 \\
 -1 & -2 \\
 1 & 2 \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and the vector
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 0.12 \\
 1.99 \\
 -0.27 \\
 -0.89 \\
 1.5 \\
 -2.85 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -0.532 \\
 -0.18 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
