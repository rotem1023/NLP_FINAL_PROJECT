Problem:
Find the least squares vector given the matrix
$\left(
\begin{array}{ccccc}
 1 & -2 & 1 & 2 & 1 \\
 2 & 0 & -1 & 1 & 1 \\
 2 & 3 & 0 & 0 & 1 \\
 1 & -3 & 0 & -3 & 1 \\
 0 & -2 & -3 & -2 & 3 \\
 -3 & 2 & 0 & -3 & 1 \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and the vector
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 1.38 \\
 -0.27 \\
 -1.44 \\
 -1.26 \\
 2.08 \\
 -1.7 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -0.433 \\
 -0.384 \\
 -0.59 \\
 0.691 \\
 0.204 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
