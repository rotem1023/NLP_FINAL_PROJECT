Problem:
Find the least squares vector given the matrix
$\left(
\begin{array}{ccc}
 -2 & 0 & 1 \\
 1 & 1 & 1 \\
 -3 & 0 & 1 \\
 3 & 3 & 1 \\
 2 & 2 & -3 \\
 -3 & -3 & -3 \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and the vector
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -2.66 \\
 2.82 \\
 0.09 \\
 -1.22 \\
 1.04 \\
 -0.16 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 0.342 \\
 -0.23 \\
 -0.122 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
