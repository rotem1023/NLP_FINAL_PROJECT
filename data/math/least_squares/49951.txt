Problem:
Find the least squares vector given the matrix
$\left(
\begin{array}{ccc}
 -1 & -3 & 1 \\
 1 & -2 & -2 \\
 -3 & 2 & 1 \\
 3 & -1 & -3 \\
 -2 & -2 & -3 \\
 -1 & 3 & -1 \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and the vector
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -1.79 \\
 2.52 \\
 -2.61 \\
 -1.21 \\
 -2.86 \\
 -0.83 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 0.731 \\
 0.046 \\
 0.361 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
