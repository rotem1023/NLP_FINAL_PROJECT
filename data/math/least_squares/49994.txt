Problem:
Find the least squares vector given the matrix
$\left(
\begin{array}{cc}
 -1 & 3 \\
 2 & 1 \\
 -2 & 1 \\
 -1 & -1 \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and the vector
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -1.57 \\
 -2.42 \\
 1.96 \\
 -0.68 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -0.751 \\
 -0.499 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
