Problem:
Find the least squares vector given the matrix
$\left(
\begin{array}{cccc}
 -1 & 3 & -1 & 0 \\
 1 & 3 & -2 & 1 \\
 -2 & -3 & 0 & 1 \\
 0 & 1 & 2 & -2 \\
 1 & 1 & 2 & 1 \\
 -3 & 2 & -2 & -3 \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and the vector
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 2.9 \\
 0.98 \\
 2.76 \\
 -0.1 \\
 0.14 \\
 0.26 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -1.331 \\
 0.498 \\
 0.368 \\
 1.127 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
