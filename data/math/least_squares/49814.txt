Problem:
Find the least squares vector given the matrix
$\left(
\begin{array}{cc}
 0 & -1 \\
 3 & -1 \\
 -1 & 2 \\
 3 & 2 \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and the vector
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -2.01 \\
 0.08 \\
 1.35 \\
 -1.98 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -0.377 \\
 0.105 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
