Problem:
Find the least squares vector given the matrix
$\left(
\begin{array}{ccc}
 -2 & -3 & 2 \\
 -2 & -2 & 1 \\
 1 & 2 & 1 \\
 -3 & 3 & -2 \\
 -2 & 0 & 3 \\
 1 & -1 & -2 \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and the vector
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 0.52 \\
 2.03 \\
 0.61 \\
 -2.32 \\
 2.57 \\
 -1.67 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 0.072 \\
 -0.053 \\
 0.841 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
