Problem:
Find the least squares vector given the matrix
$\left(
\begin{array}{ccc}
 -1 & -3 & -2 \\
 -2 & 0 & -2 \\
 3 & -3 & -2 \\
 -3 & 1 & 2 \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and the vector
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -1.22 \\
 1.12 \\
 2.14 \\
 2.33 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -0.101 \\
 -0.195 \\
 0.169 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
