Problem:
Find the least squares vector given the matrix
$\left(
\begin{array}{cc}
 -3 & -1 \\
 -2 & 0 \\
 2 & 0 \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and the vector
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 1.04 \\
 -2.36 \\
 -1.87 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 0.122 \\
 -1.408 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
