Problem:
Find the least squares vector given the matrix
$\left(
\begin{array}{ccccc}
 -2 & 1 & 1 & -2 & -1 \\
 1 & 1 & 1 & 2 & 0 \\
 0 & 2 & 1 & 2 & 3 \\
 1 & 2 & 0 & -3 & 3 \\
 0 & -1 & 2 & -2 & 3 \\
 0 & 1 & 3 & 0 & -2 \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and the vector
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -1.35 \\
 -0.61 \\
 -0.14 \\
 2.82 \\
 -1.33 \\
 2.53 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 1.288 \\
 0.693 \\
 0.037 \\
 -0.454 \\
 -0.422 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
