Problem:
Find the least squares vector given the matrix
$\left(
\begin{array}{ccc}
 3 & 2 & 2 \\
 -1 & -1 & 2 \\
 -1 & -2 & -1 \\
 3 & -2 & 1 \\
 1 & 2 & 1 \\
 -2 & 2 & 2 \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and the vector
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -1.68 \\
 1.1 \\
 -1.56 \\
 2.48 \\
 -1.62 \\
 1. \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -0.12 \\
 -0.566 \\
 0.559 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
