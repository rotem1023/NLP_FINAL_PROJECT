Problem:
Find the least squares vector given the matrix
$\left(
\begin{array}{ccc}
 2 & -2 & -1 \\
 -1 & -2 & -3 \\
 -3 & 3 & -2 \\
 1 & -3 & 0 \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and the vector
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 1.66 \\
 1.62 \\
 0.83 \\
 1.17 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 0.265 \\
 -0.094 \\
 -0.704 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
