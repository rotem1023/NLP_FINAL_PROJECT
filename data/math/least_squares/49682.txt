Problem:
Find the least squares vector given the matrix
$\left(
\begin{array}{cc}
 -3 & -1 \\
 -3 & 2 \\
 -1 & 1 \\
 -1 & 3 \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and the vector
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 0.96 \\
 -1.98 \\
 2.57 \\
 -0.29 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -0.043 \\
 -0.235 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
