Problem:
Find the least squares vector given the matrix
$\left(
\begin{array}{cccc}
 -1 & 0 & 3 & 3 \\
 1 & 1 & 1 & 0 \\
 3 & 0 & -2 & 2 \\
 2 & 3 & 2 & 3 \\
 -1 & 0 & -1 & 2 \\
 2 & 3 & -1 & 3 \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and the vector
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -1.17 \\
 1.37 \\
 2.67 \\
 -1.69 \\
 1.32 \\
 -2.4 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 0.661 \\
 -1.122 \\
 -0.139 \\
 0.133 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
