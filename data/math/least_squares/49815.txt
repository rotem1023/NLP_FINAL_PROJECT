Problem:
Find the least squares vector given the matrix
$\left(
\begin{array}{ccc}
 3 & 1 & 3 \\
 -1 & -2 & 0 \\
 3 & 1 & 0 \\
 -3 & -2 & 1 \\
 -3 & 2 & -2 \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and the vector
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -1.9 \\
 -2.3 \\
 2.87 \\
 0.75 \\
 -0.33 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 0.308 \\
 -0.058 \\
 -0.583 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
