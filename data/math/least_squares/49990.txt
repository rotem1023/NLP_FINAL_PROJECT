Problem:
Find the least squares vector given the matrix
$\left(
\begin{array}{cc}
 1 & -1 \\
 3 & 0 \\
 -3 & 1 \\
 2 & 3 \\
 -3 & 1 \\
 0 & 0 \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and the vector
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -1.72 \\
 -0.63 \\
 1.7 \\
 1.5 \\
 2.71 \\
 -1.88 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -0.406 \\
 0.852 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
