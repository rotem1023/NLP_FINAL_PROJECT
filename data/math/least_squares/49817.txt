Problem:
Find the least squares vector given the matrix
$\left(
\begin{array}{ccc}
 1 & -2 & -1 \\
 2 & 3 & -3 \\
 2 & 1 & -1 \\
 2 & 3 & -3 \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and the vector
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -1.77 \\
 2.49 \\
 1. \\
 -2.7 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 0.776 \\
 0.665 \\
 1.217 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
