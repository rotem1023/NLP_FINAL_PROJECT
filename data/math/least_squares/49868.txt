Problem:
Find the least squares vector given the matrix
$\left(
\begin{array}{ccccc}
 1 & 3 & -3 & 1 & -2 \\
 1 & -1 & -3 & 3 & 2 \\
 3 & -2 & 1 & 1 & 0 \\
 1 & 3 & -2 & 0 & 0 \\
 0 & 3 & -2 & -2 & -2 \\
 2 & -3 & -2 & -1 & -1 \\
\end{array}
\right)$ and the vector
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 1.72 \\
 -1.07 \\
 -1.82 \\
 0.7 \\
 -1.58 \\
 -1.34 \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\left(
\begin{array}{c}
 -0.534 \\
 0.169 \\
 0.151 \\
 0.643 \\
 -0.537 \\
\end{array}
\right)$
